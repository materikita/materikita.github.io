1. One hundred out of 400 lollipops are apple flavored. What percentage of the lollipops are apple flavored?


Jawaban:

25%

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

One Hundred (100) out of total 400 lollipops are apple-flavoured.

it means that:

= 100 ÷ 400 × 100%

= 25% (0.25 in decimals)

a quarter (or 25%) of lollipops are apple-flavoured.


2. One hundred out of 400 lollipops are apple flavored. What percentage of the lollipops are apple flavored?


Jawaban:

100/400 = 1/4

1/4 = 25/100 = 25%

Jawaban:

The percentage of the lollipops are apple flavored is 25%

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

All lollipops is 400 = 100%

The lollipops are apple flavored is 100 = ?%

[tex] \frac{100}{400} = \frac{100 \div 4}{400 \div 4} = \frac{25}{100} = 25\%[/tex]
