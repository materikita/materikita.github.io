Disagreement Expressions

35. Tolong di bantu ya tentang affirmative agreement and negative agreement


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: similarities and differences
Pembahasan:

12. and you have too (affirmative)
13. and so is hers. (affirmative)
14. and neither are her friends (negative)
15. and neither have you. (negative)
16. and she didn't either (negative)
17. so do the boys (affirmative)
18. we do too (affirmative)
19. neither has mine (negative)
20. and I didn't either (negative)
21. neither will the faculty (negative)
22. Mica doesn't either. (negative)
23. so does Alice (affirmative)
24. Mia does too (affirmative)
25. neither has Mia (negative)

The key:
affirmative agreement: ...so + auxiliary verb + subject
....subject + positive auxiliary verb + too
negative agreement: ...neither + auxiliary verb + subject
.... subject + negative auxiliary verb + either
