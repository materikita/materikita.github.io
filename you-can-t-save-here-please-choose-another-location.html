7. Choose 1 short story from Edgar Allan Poe or another famous writers and attach the story here​


Jawaban:

A Dream Within a Dream

BY EDGAR ALLAN POE

Take this kiss upon the brow!

And, in parting from you now,

Thus much let me avow —

You are not wrong, who deem

That my days have been a dream;

Yet if hope has flown away

In a night, or in a day,

In a vision, or in none,

Is it therefore the less gone?  

All that we see or seem

Is but a dream within a dream.

I stand amid the roar

Of a surf-tormented shore,

And I hold within my hand

Grains of the golden sand —

How few! yet how they creep

Through my fingers to the deep,

While I weep — while I weep!

O God! Can I not grasp

Them with a tighter clasp?

O God! can I not save

One from the pitiless wave?

Is all that we see or seem

But a dream within a dream?

24. Can you find another expressions to get someone's attention?


Getting someone’s attention artinya adalah meminta perhatian dari seseorang ketika sedang melakukan aktivitas yang lain atau sedang tidak memperhatikan. Beberapa ungkapan yang bisa digunakan to get someone’s attention adalah:


1. “Excuse me, Sir. You forgot to take your debit card from the ATM.” (maaf pak, anda lupa mengambil kembali kartu debit anda dari mesin ATM)

2. “Hey, I have something to tell you.” (Hai, aku mau ngomong sesuatu)

3. “Sorry, please step aside so I could pass my trolley” (Maaf, bisa minggir sebentar supaya saya dan trolli saya bisa lewat)

4. “Attention please! Is anybody here driving Black Toyota Avanza? Your alarm’s ringing!” (Perhatian semuanya! Disini ada yang mobilnya Toyota Avanza hitam? Alarm mobilmu berbunyi!)

5. “Hey, I think you’ve got something stuck in your teeth.” (Kayaknya ada sesuatu yang nyangkut di gigimu)


Kelas: SMA

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kategori: Expressions

Kata Kunci: Attention, Getting someone's attention

Kode: 10.5.2
