30. struktur gramatika "management is composed of a team of managers who have responbilities in the organization at all levels"


Answer:

Passive form is a way to make a statement focusing on the object rather than the subject itself. Such form is applicable in each tense. The common form of it is as follows
Object + be + V3 + complement

In the sentence above, gramatically it is the passive form of present tense.
Object = management
be / auxiliary verb = is 
verb 3 = composed
complement = a team of managers.......all levels. 
 
The complement is a phrase with pronoun. So technically it's a complex sentence. 
'a team of managers' is explained by pronoun 'who have responsibilities in the organization at all levels.'

Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: passive, pronoun
