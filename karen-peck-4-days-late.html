7! = 7 × 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

2.520 × 2 = 5.040

5.040 × 1 = 5.040

12 × 2 = 24

27. mesya was late coming to school too days ago ( + ) ( - ) ( ? )​


Jawaban:

mesya was late coming to school too days ago ( + )

Penjelasan:

Because  this sentence didn't have negative/interrogative sentence

Jawaban:

Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Mesya was late coming to school two days ago.

(-) Mesya was not late coming to school two days ago.

(?) Was Mesya late coming to school two days ago?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Past Continuous Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kata "late" dalam kalimat berfungsi sebagai Adverb (kata keterangan) yang memodifikasi Verb (kata kerja) "coming" dan posisi yang benar dalam kalimat: Mesya was coming late to school two days ago.

Jawaban di atas hanya mengikuti apa yang dihadirkan pada soal.

Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-ing + Adverb (late) + Object + Time Signal

(-) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + not + Verb-ing + Adverb (late) + Object + Time Signal

(?) Tobe (was/were) + Subject + Verb-ing + Adverb (late) + Object + Time Signal + ?

Semoga membantu ya.
