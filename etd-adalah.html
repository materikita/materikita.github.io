1. Apa itu STA,STD,ETA,ETD,ATA,ATD Dalam dunia penerbangan


Berikut ini beberapa pengertian dari istilah-istilah yang ada di dunia penerbangan.

STA, Scheduled Time Of Arrival, jadwal pasti kedatangan pesawat yang didasari oleh jadwal tetap.STD Schedule Time of Departure, jadwal pasti keberangkatan pesawat yang didasari oleh jadwal tetap pesawat.ETA, Estimation Time of Arrival, perkiraan waktu kedatangan pesawat.ETD, Estimation Time of Departure, perkiraan waktu keberangkatan pesawat. ATA, Actual Time Arrival, waktu pasti kedatangan pesawat.ATD, Actual Time Departure waktu keberangkatan pesawat.

Pembahasan

Dalam dunia penerbangan, banyak sekali istilah-istilah yang digunakan. Salah satu tujuan penggunaan istilah bertujuan untuk memudahkan komunikasi antara pilot atau dengan penumpang. Selain istilah-istilah di atas, berikut ini istilah-istilah lainnya yang sering digunakan pada dunia penerbangan lainnya.

Gate, merupakan pintu masuk menuju pesawat terbang.Delay, keterlambatan baik keberangkatan maupun kedatangan pesawat.FA, Flight Attentandt, merupakan istilah untuk pramugari dan pramugara.Booking, dapat diartikan sebagai proses pemesanan dan pembelian tiket.Child, diartikan sebagai anak-anak dengan usia 2 hingga 12 tahun.Landing, pesawat berhasil mendarat.Take Off, pesawat akan terbang dari jalur landasan.Infant, penumpang bayi dengan usia 0 hingga 24 bulan.Reservasi, program pemesanan tiket perjalanan melalui online atau pemesanan secara offline.Window Seat, letak kursi pada pesawat yang berdekatan dengan jendela.

Dengan memahami istilah-istilah dalam dunia penerbangan, maka kesalahan dalam bepergian menggunakan pesawat terbang dapat diminimalisir.

2. Apa itu STA,STD,ETA,ETD,ATA,ATD Dalam dunia penerbangan


Apa itu STA, STD, ETA, ETD, ATA, ATD Dalam dunia penerbangan

STA adalah Standard Terminal Arrival RouteSTD adalah StandardETA adalah Estimated Time Of Arrival atau yang diartikan sebagai waktu perkiraan kedatanganETD adalah Estimated Time of Departure atau yang diartikan sebagai waktu perkiraan keberangkatanATA adalah Actual Time of Arrival atau yang diartikan sebagai waktu pasti kedatanganATD adalah Actual Time of Departure atau yang diartikan sebagai waktu pasti keberangkatanPembahasan  

Penerbangan umum atau yang dimana disebut pula dengan sebutan general aviation merupaakn salah satu dari dua macam bentuk kategori dari penerbangan sipil. Hal ini kemudian akan menuju kepada segala macam bentuk penerbangan yang dimana akan disebut selain dari penerbangan militer dan juga maskapai terjadwal dan juga kargo reguler, apakah hal tersebut pribadi dan juga komersial.

Jawaban:

1. A hotel representative will meet you at the airport when you arrive. What's your Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA)?  

2. After several guests complained about poor quality in the restaurant, the hotel hired a new Food & Beverage (F&B) manager.  

3. I've checked our computer and I'm afraid there's no Passenger Name Record (PNR) for you. Are you sure you have the correct flight details there?  

4. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) in the USA has banned passengers from taking sharp objects on board aircraft, but mysteriously still allows them to take potentially far more lethal glass bottles on board  

5. As part of airport security, closed-circuit television (CCTV) cameras are being installed in all the terminals.  

6. This is an Round The World (RTW) air ticket, beginning and ending in London, and going via Dubai, Hong Kong, Sydney, San Francisco and New York.  

7. In order to improve their quality of service, most tour operators ask their customers to fill in a Customer Survey Questionnaire (CSQ) at the end of their holiday.  

8. Passengers who check-in late for their flight might be allowed through to the departure gate if they hurry, but most airlines try to discourage these have-a-go (HAG) travelers.  

9. Some restaurants are attempting to entice customers by offering a Bring Your Own (BYO) option, and charging a small fee for opening the bottle.  

10. I'm afraid the flight has been delayed. The new Estimated Time of Departure (ETD) is 21.30, and we hope to begin boarding at 20.45.  

11. Passengers who experience long delays at airports are entitled to an light refreshment voucher (LVR) from their airline so that they can get something to eat and drink.  

12. Most travel agencies have a computer reservation system (CRS) to assist them in booking flights, hotel rooms, holidays, etc, for their customers.  

13. I bought a cheap Advance Purchase Excursion (APEX) air ticket to Madrid.  

14. We stayed at a lovely Bed & Breakfast (B&B) last week. The room was large and comfortable, and the breakfast was excellent.  

15. For many hotels, their main Point of Sale (POS) for accommodation is now the Internet, but many still rely on travel agents to sell their rooms.  

16. This ticket says One Way (OW). If you want to come back, you need one that says Round Trip (RT).  

17. The tour operator isn't very well known. What it needs is a really good Public Relations Officer (PRO).  

18. The RRP (Recommended Retail Price) was £75, but I got it for only £25 in the sale.  

19. It's very urgent: can you contact her ASAP (As Soon As Possible)?  

20. First class and Business class passengers get free entry to the VIP (Very Important Person) suite at the airport.  

21. The villa is halfway up a mountain, so you'll need a good 4WD (4-Wheel Drive) vehicle to get there and back.  

22. Singapore is 8 hours ahead of GMT (Greenwich Mean Time), so when it's midday in London, it's 8 pm in Singapore.  

23. The hotel has IDD (International Direct Dialling) telephone facilities, so you don't need to go through the hotel switchboard when you want to make an international phone call.  

24. The airline has a FFP (Frequent Flyer Programme) which offers several rewards and benefits to passengers who use them on a regular basis.  

25. I need to get some cash. Is there an Automated Teller Machine (ATM) near the hotel?  

26. The EHA (Environmental Health Officer) closed the hotel when he found a rat in the kitchen and two dead pigeons in the water tank.  

27. Visitors to the national park are reminded that this is an (ESA)  

Environmentally Sensitive Area, and they should therefore follow all the rules set out at the entrance.  

28. Many Americans prefer going on an FET (Foreign Escorted Tour) when they go abroad, as they feel more secure when someone is there to help them.  

29. The advantage of a GDS (Global Distribution System) such as Galileo International or AMADEUS is that it is very easy for a passenger who is in Japan (for example) to book a flight from Rome to Edinburgh.  

30. The airline has decided to move its HQ (Headquarter) from Heathrow Airport to Stansted Airport to help reduce overheads.  

31. When you book a flight on the Internet, you usually receive a receipt for an E (Electronic)-ticket via your email.  

32. The hotel is at 27 Lombard Street in Boston, but I don't know the ZIP (Zone Improvement Plan) code.  

33. The hotel chain spent $20m on renovation work, so they are hoping to get a good ROI (Return on Investment).  

34. The American and Canadian time zone on the east side of those countries is known as EST (Eastern Standard Time). The other time zones in these countries are PST (Pacific Standard Time), MST (Mountain Standard Time), CST (Central Standard Time) and (in Canada only) AST (Atlantic Standard Time) (also called Provincial Standard Time).
