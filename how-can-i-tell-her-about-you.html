21. Can you tell me about conditional sentence


Jawaban:

A conditional sentence is a sentence that has two parts: the condition and the result. The condition is a statement about a possible situation. The result is a statement about what would happen if the condition is true.

If you study hard, you will pass the test. This is a conditional sentence. The condition is "if you study hard." The result is "you will pass the test."

In a type 1 conditional sentence, the tense in the "if" clause is the simple present, and the tense in the main clause is the simple future

In a type 1 conditional sentence, the tense in the "if" clause is the simple present, and the tense in the main clause is the simple futureIn a type 2 conditional sentence, the tense in the "if" clause is the simple past, and the tense in the main clause is the present conditional or the present continuous conditional.

In a type 1 conditional sentence, the tense in the "if" clause is the simple present, and the tense in the main clause is the simple futureIn a type 2 conditional sentence, the tense in the "if" clause is the simple past, and the tense in the main clause is the present conditional or the present continuous conditional.in a type 3 , if certain terms (conditionals, limitations) had been met.

Penjelasan:

type 1

if + simple present, simple future “will” / imperative

ex : If you rest, you will feel better.

type 2

if + simple past/were, would/could/might + bare infinitive.

ex : If I owned a zoo, I might let people interact with the animals more.

type 3

if + past perfect, would/should/could/might + have + past participle.

ex : If you had remembered to invite me, I would have attended your party.
