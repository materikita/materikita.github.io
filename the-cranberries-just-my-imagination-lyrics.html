A song is defined as a short musical composition of words and music. Lyrics are words that make up a song. We can use songs to practice lots of different language skills. In this case, we can leam English through songs. We would like to learn about how a song is organized (structure) and the social function of songs.

• How to Understand a Song

Song must have meaning. To understand a song, we should know the meaning. The following are ways to figure out a song’s meaning.

A. Listen to the song while reading the lyrics.

B. Read the lyrics on their own to interpret their meaning. You will find the speaker, the word / you, or we refer to.

C. Focus on concrete imagery and sense. Lyrics describe a sense of feeling with images, not just vague ideas or phrases.

D. Unpack or expose unusual or poetic phrases one at time, for example the use of figurative language. They are when the words don't literally mean what they say.
