Hence, when you tell people that they are "too skinny" or to "eat more", even if it's just for fun, that means you are praising them and that they shouldn't feel offended by your words. Someone in a thin body—particularly a woman—may be teased, bullied, or discriminated against for not possessing a certain body type historically associated with femininity and the male gaze.
