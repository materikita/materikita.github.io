Jawaban:

Present perfect tense.

1. I have greeted my teacher and friends in English.

2. I have tried my best to use English with my English teacher.

3. All the way to school this morning, I have tried to talk to Budi in English.

4. I have asked my teacher the English word for 'melamun'. It is 'daydream'.

Penjelasan:

Present perfect tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk kejadian atau peristiwa yang berlangsung di waktu lampau dan belum selesai pada saat dibicarakan.

Rumus verbal present perfect tense adalah

subject + have/has + verb 3.

Rumus nominal present perfect tense adalah

subject + have/has + been + noun/adjective/adverb.
