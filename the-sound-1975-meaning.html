Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Teks tersebut memiliki sifat komparatif digunakan untuk membandingkan perbedaan antara dua objek.

Analisis, perhatikan:

1. Faster (Artinya ini ada komparasi antara Light waves dan sound waves), jika ada faster, kita tambahkan "than" setelahnya.

2. Dan Verb yang digunakan adalah “travel” maka auxiliary yang digunakan adalah "do/does"

3. So, we see the lightening before we hear the thunder = Jadi, kita melihat kilat sebelum mendengar guntur.

Jadi artinya Light waves travel faster than sound waves do = Gelombang cahaya merambat lebih cepat daripada gelombang suara.

23. How is about the sound of the guitar​


Jawaban:

Strings are run from the neck to the body. When a string is plucked its vibration is transmitted from the bridge, resonating throughout the top of the guitar. It is also transmitted to the side and back of the instrument, resonating through the air in the body, finally producing sound from the sound hole.

Jawaban:

Guitars consist of two sections: the neck and the body. Strings are run from the neck to the body. When a string is plucked its vibration is transmitted from the bridge, resonating throughout the top of the guitar. It is also transmitted to the side and back of the instrument, resonating through the air in the body, finally producing sound from the sound hole.
