18. how ......... are these bananas ? ( much / many )​


"much "karena tdk diketahui jumlahnya

jika disebutkan jumlahnya bisa gunakan "many"

Soal:

How ......... are these bananas ?

( much / many )​

Jawaban:

Much

Penjelasan:

✅ Karena MANY utk kata benda yg bisa dihitung / COUNTABLE

✅ Sedangkan MUCH utk kata benda yg tak bisa dihitung / UNCOUNTABLE

✅ karena soal menanyakan harga (yg belum diketahui) maka menggunakan MUCH

Jadi:

How MUCH are these bananas ?

= Berapa HARGA pisang-pisang ini?

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Uncountable and Countable
