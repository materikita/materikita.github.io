28. . Answer the questions using Neither ... nor 1. Her husband doesn't speak English. Do her children speak English? 2. She doesn't enjoy hunting Does she enjoy fishing​


Jawaban:

1. Her husband doesn't speak English. Do her children speak English?

: Neither her husband nor her children don't speak English.

2. She doesn't enjoy hunting Does she enjoy fishing

She doesn't enjoy neither hunting nor fishing.

Penjelasan:

Neither ... Nor itu arti nya tidak kedua nya, nor semacam kata 'and' tapi khusus neither, yang di gunakan nor.

Cara tau nya, subject atau kata kerja nya ada 2 itu yg di kasi neither ... nor.

Kaya no 1, subject nya ada 2 kan, her husband dan her children. Maka neither nya masuk sebelum her husband her children, sedangkan nor masuk di antara her husband her children.

No 2, kata kerja nya yang ada 2, hunting dan fishing. Sama kaya penjelasan di atas.
