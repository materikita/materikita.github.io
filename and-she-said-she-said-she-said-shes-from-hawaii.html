38. 1. Direct = She said, " I bought a pen. "Indirect = ( She said that she had bought a pen / she said that she had had bought a pen ) ?2. Direct = He said, " My family will come from Japan. "Indirect = ?tolong berikan penjelasan dari kalimat langsung dan kalimat tidak langsung diatas ya dan jawab dengan benar


((Direct adalah kalimat langsung dalam bahasa Indonesia; sedangkan, Indirect adalah kalimat tidak langsung. Sebenarnya, cara melakukannya pun sama dengan yang ada dalam bahasa Indonesia))

Direct: She said, "I bought a pen,"
((Langsung: Dia-perempuan- berkata, "Aku membeli sebuah pulpen"))

Indirect: (She said that she had bought a pen/ she said that she had had bought a pen)
((Tidak langsung: Ia berkata Ia membeli sebuah pulpen / Ia berkata Ia telah membeli sebuah pulpen))

2. Direct: He said, "My family will come from Japan."
((Langsung: Ia berkata, "Keluargaku akan datang dari Jepang."))

((Indirect: He had said that his family will come from Japan; atau dalam bahasa Indonesia, 'Dia telah berkata keluarganya akan datang dari Jepang))
2. Direct, kalimat langung. Ciri cirinya pake tanda kutip. 

He said " My family will come from Japan "

Indirect, kalimat tak langung. Kalimatnya diceritakan lewat seseorang, bukan kata sendirinya. Ga pake tanda kutip. Pake 'that'. Pronoun nya berubah.
