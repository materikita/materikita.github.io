PRESENT PERFECT

My wife and I have travelled as much as possible since we retired. We've visited cousins in Australia, and we have been to New Zealand twice. We've also gone on safari in Africa, We've been to Europe a lot. Gina and I have spent time in Paris, in Madrid, and in several cities in Italy. We haven't been to Eastern Europe yet, though. We hope to visit Prague and Budapest next year.

My sister Betty was born in the United States, and she has never travelled outside the country-except once when she was very young. But she has been to a lot of places in the U.S. She has visited most of the national parks: the Grand Canyon, Yellowstone, Yosemite, and so on. She has been to all the big cities, too. In fact, she has lived in four different cities in the U.S.: New York, Boston, Los Angeles, and San Francisco. I think she has seen more of her own country than most people.

-----------------

EXPLANATION

Present perfect tense adalah bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang dimulai di suatu waktu yang tidak spesifik di masa lalu (past) tetapi mempunyai hubungan dengan masa saat pernyataan tersebut diutarakan (present).

Pada present perfect, kapan waktu tepatnya suatu aksi dilakukan tidaklah penting.

Present perfect ditandai dengan penggunaan kata-kata seperti: ever, never, once, several times, already, yet, since ..., for ..., so far, dll.

Present perfect digunakan untuk:

1. menceritakan pengalaman  

e.g I have been to London.

2. membicarakan tentang suatu perubahan yang terjadi dalam suatu periode waktu

e.g. He has grown taller.

3. membicarakan pencapaian seseorang atau sekelompok orang

e.g. Scientists have found cure for cancer.

4. membicarakan sesuatu yang diharapkan tetapi belum terjadi

e.g Scientists still haven't found the cure for cancer.

5. membicarakan suatu aksi yang terjadi berulang kali pada saat yang berbeda-beda

e.g. I have seen that film six times.

6. membicarakan sesutau yang dimulai di masa lalu tetapi terus terjadi di masa sekarang

e.g. They have been married for five years.

Konstruksi kalimat dalam present perfect adalah:

(+) S + have/has + past participle (v₃)

(-) S + have/has + not + past participle

(?) have/has + S + past participle

Auxiliary 'have' digunakan jika subjek merupakan orang pertama (I), orang kedua (you), atau jamak (they).

Auxiliary 'has' digunakan jika subjek merupakan orang ketiga tunggal (he/she/it).
