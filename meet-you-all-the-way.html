When you meet someone on the way, you can greet them with a friendly "Hello!" or "Hi!" If you want to be more polite, you can say Good morning/afternoon/evening depending on the time of day. Additionally, you can use phrases like "How are you?" or "Nice to see you!" to show interest in their well-being or express friendliness.
