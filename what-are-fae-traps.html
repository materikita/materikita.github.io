1. My laptop is repaired / was repaired last week.

2. The first computers are invented / were invented a long time ago.

3. In 1996, the first chess match is played / was played between the world chess champion and a  computer.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat pada jawaban di atas menggunakan bentuk Passive Voice dari Simple Present Tense dan Simple Past Tense.

Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

Semoga membantu ya.
