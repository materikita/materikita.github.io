VAT = Value Added Tax. In indonesia its called Pajak Pertambahan Nilai (PPN).

Michelle bought a car and she got price off 15% from Car Price and exclude VAT 10% from Car Price.

1. If The VAT is calculate from first car amount (Before Discount) :

So, Total Discount that michelle's get : 15% - 10% = 5%.

She Paid with amount $22.440 after she gets 5% discount. so, $22.440 is 95/100 part from car amount.

so the Car amount is = 100/95 x $22.440

The Car Amount is = $23.578,9 or i'm rounded the amount to = $23.579.

2. If The VAT is Calculate from car amount After it gets discount :

The car amount before VAT is Calculated :

100%/(100%+10%) x $22.440

= 100/110 x $22.440

= $20.400

Car Amount = x.

100%x - 15%x = $20.400

85%x = $20.400

x = $20.400 x 100/85

x = $24.000

So, the car amount in Possibility 2 is = $24.000

VAT is value-added tax.

Let the price of car (before price-off) is [tex]~x[/tex]

There is a condition : excluding VAT 10%, so :

1/10 x 85/100

[tex]\begin{array}{rcl}(x-[15\%\times x])+(10\%\times \{x-[15\%\times x]\})&=&22,440\\~\\(85\%\times x)+(8,5\%\times x)&=&22,440\\~\\93,5\%\times x&=&22,440\\~\\x&=&22,440\times \frac{100}{93,5}\end{array}[/tex]

So, the price of car is [tex]~\red{\huge \$24,000}[/tex]

Bullying (disebut "Bullying/Rising" dalam bahasa Indonesia) segala bentuk penindasan atau kekerasan sengaja oleh orang atau kelompok yang lebih kuat menyakiti orang lain, atau dilakukan secara terus menerus. Bullying memiliki banyak definisi, terutama yang terjadi di setting lain seperti rumah, pekerjaan, masyarakat, dan komunitas. maya. Namun dalam hal ini dibatasi dalam kerangka school bullying atau school bullying - bullying di sekolah. Pembahasan Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutinitas dan fakta umum.

Adverb of frequency Always, Often, diletakkan di antara verbSometimes ➡️ diletakkan di awal dan akhir kalimat

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + adv.freq + V1 (-s/es) + Complement

(-) S + do/does + not + Adv.freq + V1 + Complement

(?) Do/does + S + Adv.freq + V1 + Complement?

Kesimpulan

Huruf acak dalam soal terdapat huruf kapital sebagai penanda kata pertama:

1. I usually get up early in the morning
