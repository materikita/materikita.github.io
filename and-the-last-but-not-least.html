'I may not be perfect but I am not fake.'

4. what is the most and least effective powder to neutralize hydrochloride acid?


Jawaban:

The most effective powder to neutralize hydrochloric acid is sodium hydroxide (NaOH), also known as lye. Sodium hydroxide is a strong base and can effectively neutralize hydrochloric acid to form sodium chloride (NaCl) and water.

The least effective powder to neutralize hydrochloric acid is baking soda (sodium bicarbonate, NaHCO3), which is a weak base. While it can partially neutralize hydrochloric acid, it may not neutralize it completely, and the reaction may produce carbon dioxide gas, making it less effective than sodium hydroxide.

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Materi: Present Perfect Tense

Jawaban:

C. Spent

Penjelasan:

Present Perfect Tense adalah bentuk waktu yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kalimat yang telah/sudah saja terjadi.

Dari opsi tersebut, kombinasi kalimat paling sempurna adalah dengan kata kerja menghabiskan. Yaitu kalimat utuhnya:

"And I've spent at least six weeks there in the last year"

"Dan aku telah menghabiskan setidaknya enam minggu disana tahun lalu"

Dengan menggunakan rumus Present Perfect Tense:

S + have/has + v3 (past participle verb) + O

Maka dipilih kata kerja menghabiskan bentuk past participle, yaitu C. Spent.

40.  find example of suggestions and offer in the story"The Enchanted Fish" at least five


The Enchanted Fish is a story written by Brother's Grimm. We can find several examples of suggestion and offer in this story. In order to find sentences that indicate suggestion and offer, there are some different samples of expressions for suggestion and offer which you can use to find at least five suggestion and offer in the story.

Discussion

Suggestion and offer are very similar. Both suggestion and offer are ideas that we give to others for consideration. People can accept or reject the idea we give to them. A sentence that indicates a suggestion/offer usually uses the modal verb 'should' and 'shall'. Another common phrases that we can use for a suggestion/offer are 'what about + verb -ing', 'how about + verb -ing'. Here are some examples of a suggestion and offer.

- I think you should go to the headmaster office as soon as possible.

- What about working on our presentation right after we finish our lunch?

- Shall I ask the director on your behalf?

- May I help you?

- Would you like me to help?

- Would you like some drink?

Answers
