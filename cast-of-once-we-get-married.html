5. we re getting married.....march​


we're getting married in march

Pembahasan : Use IN when :

- Talking about longer periods of time like months, years and parts of the day - Jangka waktu yang lebih laman

- the person speaking is inside that place

Example :

■ In july, In the morning

■ I eat dinner in the dining room

Use ON when

- talking about specific date and days of the week tanggal atau hari tertentu

- Spesial day

- Surfaces - diatas permukaan

Example :

■ On weekends

■ On my birthday

■ on the table

Use AT when :

- the exact time you do the activity use AT - waktu yang pasti melakukan aktivitas

Example :

■ I wake up at 7 o'clock

duster= y
