3. Hannah said,” This is my favourite book and I want to give it to _____ (yours/you). Now it’s _____(yours/you).”


Kelas : X
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : pronouns
Kata kunci : possessive pronoun, subjective pronoun, objective pronoun

Pembahasan:
Pronoun disebut juga dengan kata ganti. Possessive pronoun digunakan sebagai kata ganti milik seseorang. Fungsi dari pronoun adalah untuk menghindari terjadinya pengulangan kata benda atau nama. Possessive pronoun diikuti oleh kata benda yang kepemilikannya mengacu pada subject atau subjective pronoun.

Subjective Pronoun terdiri dari I, You, We, They, She, He, It.
Objective pronoun terdiri Me, You, Us, Them, Her, His, It.
Possessive adjective terdiri dari My, Your, Our, Their, Her, His, Its.
Possessive pronoun terdiri dari Mine, Yours, Ours, Theirs, Hers, His, Its.

Hannah said,” This is my favourite book and I want to give it to YOU.
⇒ 'You' merupakan objective pronoun yang pada soal terletak sebagai object. 

Now it’s YOURS."
⇒ 'Yours' merupakan possessive pronoun yang mengacu pada kata 'you' di kalimat sebelumnya, yang apabila diartikan adalah milikmu.

Jawaban:

The theme of the song "Minefields" by Faouzia ft. John Legend is the challenges and risks of being in a complicated relationship.The lyrics "these minefields that I walk through, what I risk to be close to you" mean that the singer is willing to go through difficult and dangerous situations just to be with the person they love. The "minefields" can represent the obstacles and challenges that the singer faces in the relationship, but they are willing to take the risk because their love for the other person is so strong.The lyrics "until all the lights were off" mean that the singer and the other person were together until the very end, until everything was dark and there was nothing left. It could also represent the idea of being in a relationship that is kept hidden or secret, and only taking place in the darkness or behind closed doors.
