34. Turn the following Present sentences into Past! _Reyhan always sit in front of his PC.


Reyhan sat in front of his PC.

alwaysnya hilang karena bukan present lagi.

Maaf kalau salah ʕっ•ᴥ•ʔっ

Jawaban:

Present Simple into Past Simple

Present :

Reyhan always sit in front of his PC.

Past :

Reyhan sat in front of his PC (yesterday).

Penjelasan:

Rumus Simple Present :

(+) S + V1 + s/es

(-) S + do/does + not +V1

(?) Do/does + S + V1?

Rumus Past Simple :

(+) S + V2

(-) S + did + not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1?

Perintah soal diatas :

=> Turn the following Present sentences into Past!

(Ubahlah kalimat berikut dari Present menjadi Past)

=> Dengan mengikuti rumus diatas, maka:Simple Present:

(+) Reyhan always sit in front of his PC.

(-) Reyhan doesn't always sit in front of his PC.

(?) Does Reyhan always sit in front of his PC?

Simple Past:

(+) Reyhan sat in front of his PC (yesterday).

(-) Reyhan didn't sat in front of his PC (yesterday).

(?) Did Reyhan sat in front of his PC (yesterday)?

*kata yesterday dapat ditambahkan pada Simple Past karena menunjukan kejadian tersebut sudah terjadi pada masa lampau.
