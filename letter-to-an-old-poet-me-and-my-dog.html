SIMPLE PAST TENSE

my father(2.pass)....away when i was one month old and my mother was left a single parent,to bring me up by herself.​

JAWABAN:

Karena soal merupakan kalimat dalam bentuk simple past tense yang harus menggunakan kata kerja ke-dua, maka jawabannya adalah:

=> My father passed away when I was one month old and my mother was left a single parent,to bring me up by herself.​

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils ?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

10. (+) My father was in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office  yesterday afternoon?  

1 1. (+) The floor was so dirty, I cleaned it all day

   (-) The floor was not so dirty, I didn't clean it all day

   (?) Was the floor so dirty, Did I clean it all day?

   (-)The shop did not close late yesterday
