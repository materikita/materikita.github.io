36. 6. An inverted image is magnified by 2 when the object is placed 22 cm in front of a concave mirror. Determine the image distance and the focal length of the mirror.


Answer :

M = 2, do = 22 cm

di = ...., f = ....

M = di/do

2 = di/22

di = 2 × 22

di = 44

1/f = 1/do + 1/di

1/f = 1/22 + 1/44

1/f = 2/44 + 1/44

1/f = 3/44

f = 14.66

The image distance is 44 cm and the focal length is 14.66 cm

Characteristics of the image formed by a plane mirror:-
