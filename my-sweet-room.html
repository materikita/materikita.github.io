4. Deskripsi teks my sweet home


My Lovely House

I have a house. It is actually not my house officially, but it can be said as a family house. It is a big and lovely house where my family, my grandmother and grandfather, my cousins, and also I spend our time together, especially in a big events, such as Eid or holidays. My house is located in Bogor city. I really like my house because from my house I can see how beautiful the view of Bogor city.

My house is not a big one. The area of my house is only about 200 meters square and the house itself is about 180 meters square. The design of my house is minimalis as many of houses out there, but it is also combined with a little details of old memories there, such as the windows in my house are old-fashioned ones. Sometimes, it is good to combine the old things with the new ones as the freshment. My house has two floors. The first floor has some rooms. It has living room for my family members or I welcome the guests. The living room has two big sofas, one big wooded table, and one big vase of tulip flowers, the floers that I like the most. Then, there is a family room. It is the biggest room in this house because it is the place where all if my family members gather. In this room, there are one big television hanging on the wall, one big couch and a standing lamp for those who like to read, and one big carpet. There is no sofa in this room because my parents want all of their children and also all of family members to gather and talk together in this room. This is so warm. Then, there is two bedrooms: the main bedroom for my parents and one bedroom is for my grandfather and grandmother when they come or for the guest when they want to spend the night here. The kitchen and the dining room are also in the first floor.

Whereas in the second floor, there are three bedrooms, a mini music room, a mini library, and a mini office. The bedrooms are made not as big as the rooms in the first floor because the rooms in the second floors are for me and other siblings and cousins. The mini music room is made because most of our family members like to play music. There are drum, guitar, and also a set of karaoke things. A mini library is place where I and other family member can read our favorite books in the spare time. While office room is the place where my father or I do our works without any disturbance from others.

This house is not really big, but I feel really warm here with many of my family members gather and laughs together as a strong bond.

22. my mother cooks a sweet cake for me(passive)..... ​


PASSIVE VOICE

A sweet cake is cooked {by my mother} for me

or

I am cooked {by my mother} a sweet cake

EXPLANATION

Passive voice fokus pada obyek { si penerima aksi} beserta kegiatannya daripada pemberitaan utama dari si pelaku aksi { subyek }.

Struktur umum pembentukan passive voice dengan menggunakan auxiliary verb "Be" dengan past participle verb (Verb³).

Auxiliary verb "Be" dikonjugasi berdasarkan jenis tenses masing-masing pernyataan dalam kalimat aktif.

Perubahan kalimat aktif ke kalimat pasif dengan mengubah posisi obyek kalimat aktif menjadi subyek kalimat pasif. Sedangkan, subyek { si pelaku aksi } dari kalimat aktif diposisikan sebagai obyek preposisi dengan diawali preposisi "by" pada kalimat pasif.

Contoh pembahasan pada soal yang diberikan :

A : My mother cooks a sweet cake for me

P : A sweet cake is cooked {by my mother} for me

P : I am cooked {by my mother} a sweet cake

Struktur

A ➡ S+verb1 (s/es)+O

P ➡ S+tobe(is/am/are)+Verb³+by Agent

Keterangan :

Kalimat aktif diatas menggunakan pola Simple present tense. Sehingga, pola passive voice dikonjugasi ke bentuk tenses yg sama dengan penambahan "be{ is/am/are}." Terdapat 2 bentukan kalimat pasif dikarenakan terdapat 2 objects pada kalimat aktifnya, yaitu direct object { apa yang diberikan } dan indirect object { kepada siapa direct object diberikan } Penambahan tobe { is dan am } didasarkan pada bentuk subyek yang baru / didepannya.

26. "My room gets so messy" or "my room get so messy", bedanya apa dan jelaskan


Jawaban:

My room gets so messy

Penjelasan:

karena my room  sebagai subject adalah singular

Answer:

"My room gets so messy" or "my room get so messy"

Whyyy??

A singular subject requires a singular verb, and a plural subject requires a plural verb. In this type of construction, the verb is controlled not by the word there but by the logical subject of the clause, which usually comes right after the verb.

You may have done learn about the verb + s in elementary school. If the subject; I, you, they, we, you'll use a normal verb (I eat sushi). But if the subject; she, he, it, you'll use verb + s (She eats sushi).

In this case, the right one is the first one.

(In Indonesian)

Ini tergantung subject, kalo subject nya She He It (kalo yg satu ini yang It karena my room) kita pakenya verb + s kayak sleeps, studies, writes. Kalo subject nya I, you, they, we, pake nya verb yang biasa aja gausah pake s
