Dimana Latihannya? Saya Jelaskan Materinya Yah Semoga Paham ^^

Past tense is the tense which is used when we want to express something that happened or was done in the past time. The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past.

Pendahuluan:

Past tense is the tense which is used when we want to express something that happened or was done in the past time. The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past.

Formula :

A. Helping Verb [ With Example ]

(+) S + V² + O = I Was a Teacher

(?) V² + S + O = Was I a Teacher ?

(-) S + V² + Not + O = I Was not a Teacher

B. Full Verb [ With Example ]

(+) S + V² + O = She Drew an Apple

(?) Did + S + V¹ + O = Did She Draw an Apple?

(-) S + did + not + V¹ + O = She Did not Draw an Apple

Notes :

Keterangan waktu yang biasa dipakai adalah yesterday ( kemarin ) , last ( lalu ) , dan ago ( lalu ).

Untuk V² dibagi menjadi 2 jenis yaitu :

1) Regular Verbs ( Kata Kerja Beraturan )

Catatan : adding "d" or "ed"

Open = OpenedStudy = Studied

2. Irregular Verbs ( Kata Kerja Non Beraturan )

Catatan : not adding "d" or "ed"

Draw = DrewGo = Went

Untuk membaca V² yang berakhiran "ed" pada kata kerja Regular Verbs adalah dengan cara menekan ujung lidah tanpa harus dikeluarkan/membunyikannya secara jelas.

Seperti: Passed, worked, cleaned

Tapi banyak juga, verb yang tidak bisa ditambah huruf2 tersebut atau bentuknya paten (verb 2 sudah ditentukan dari sananya)

Seperti: Woke, got, drove, made, came

37. complete the sentences in simple past form​


Pertanyaan dan Jawaban

1. It was warm, so you took off my coat (take)

Jawaban: took off

2. The film wasn't very good. I didn't enjoy it very much. (enjoy)

Jawaban: didn't enjoy

3. I knew Sarah was very busy, So I ... her. (disturb)

Jawaban asli: did not disturb

Jawaban benar: did not disturb

4. I was very tired, so I ... to bed early. (go)

Jawaban asli: went

Jawaban benar: went

5. The bed was very uncomfortable. I... very well. (sleep)

Jawaban asli: did not sleep

Jawaban benar: did not sleep

6. Sue wasn't hungry, so she ... anything. (eat)

Jawaban asli: did not eat

Jawaban benar: did not eat

7. We went to Kate's house but she ... at home. (be)

Jawaban asli: was not

Jawaban benar: was not

8. It was a funny situation but nobody .... (laugh)

Jawaban asli: laughed

Jawaban benar: laughed

9. The window was open and a bird ... into the room. (fly)

Jawaban asli: fly

Jawaban benar: flew

10. I was in a hurry, so I ... time to phone you. (have)

Jawaban asli: had

Jawaban benar: didn't have

Penjelasan

1. "Took off" adalah bentuk past tense dari "take off," sesuai dengan konteks kalimat.

2. "Didn't enjoy" sudah sesuai karena filmnya tidak bagus.

3. "Did not disturb" sesuai karena tahu Sarah sibuk.

4. "Went" adalah bentuk past tense dari "go," sesuai dengan soal.

5. "Did not sleep" cocok karena tempat tidur tidak nyaman.

6. "Did not eat" sesuai karena Sue tidak lapar.

7. "Was not" adalah bentuk past tense dari "be," sesuai dengan soal.

8. "Laughed" adalah bentuk past tense dari "laugh," sesuai dengan soal.

9. "Flew" adalah bentuk past tense dari "fly," lebih sesuai dibanding "fly."

10. "Didn't have" lebih sesuai karena dalam soal ini, tidak ada waktu untuk menelepon.
