16. change their sentence into present perfect tense:Little Terry (sing) her favorite song​


Present Perfect Tense(+) Little Terry has sung her favorite song(-) Little Terry has not sung her favorite song(?) has Little Terry sung her favorite song?

PembahasanPresent Perfect Tense

➡️ Digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang sudah selesai tapi masih ada kaitannya dengan masa sekarang.

Rumus Present Perfect

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

(-) S + has/have + not + V3 + O

(?) has/have + S + V3 + O?

CatatanI/you/we/they + have ✔️He/she/it + has
