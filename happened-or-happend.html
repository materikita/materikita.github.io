2. beda nya happend, happen, happened, sama happens apa sih? pusing nih bantu ya..


Kelas : SMA
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : verb
Kata kunci : happen, present verb, past verb, past participle verb

Pembahasan:
Happen (verb), yang membedakannya:
1. Happen dan happens digunakan untuk present tense dan future tense. Yang membedakan adalah subject-nya.
I/you/they/we + happen
She/he/it/name + happens
I/you/they/we/she/he/it + will + happen
I/you/they/we/she/he/it + am/is/are+was/were + going to + happen
Contoh:
It happens regularly.
Flood will happen if we throw rubbish everywhere.

2. Happened, digunakan untuk past tense dan past participle tense.
Contoh:
(past tense - v2) It happened last year.
(past participle - v3) That thing would have happened if I hadnot studied hard.

20. What happend when we boil water​


Jawaban:

Well, when water (or any other liquid) is boiling, the vapour pressure of the boiling water is EQUAL to the EXTERNAL pressure, and bubbles of vapour form directly in the liquid. The [math]\text{normal boiling point}[/math] is specified when the vapour pressure expressed by liquid (and thus also the AMBIENT pressure) is ONE ATMOSPHERE.

Note also that we can use this definition of boiling to explain the efficacy of vacuum distillation, when an otherwise INVOLATILE liquid is subjected to HIGH VACUUM. That is we reduce the AMBIENT pressure, and the liquid may be heated at a lower temperature such that its vapour is equal to the reduced pressure…and the liquid volatilizes and then is distilled…
