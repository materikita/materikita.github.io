1. Have she doesn't on her face dimple


REARRANGE THESE JUMBLED WORDS INTO A GOOD SENTENCE

HAVE-SHE-DOESN'T-ON-HER-FACE-DIMPLE

___________________

JAWABAN :

She doesn't have dimple on her face .

{ Dia tidak mempunya lesung pipi di wajahnya }

Penjabaran :

She => Subyek dari pronoun "dia perempuan "

doesn't => Auxiliary verb negative form "tidak"

have => Action verb "mempunyai"

dimple => noun " lesung pipi "

on => preposition " di"

her => possessive adjective "kepemilikan nya ( perempuan )

face => noun "muka/wajah."

TIPS  :

~ Cermati arti masing-masing vocabulary

~ Tentukan subyek yang tepat dan logis.

~ Lihat struktur kalimat yang diterapkan ( meliputi tenses dan bentuk kalimatnya "positive form/negative form/interrogative form)

~ Terapkan pola penyusunan kalimat sederhana "SUBJECT+VERB+OBJECT/COMPLEMENT"

_____________________

KEYWORDS = INTERPRETATION ; JUMBLED WORDS ; SENTENCE ARRANGEMENT

GRADE        = JHS
