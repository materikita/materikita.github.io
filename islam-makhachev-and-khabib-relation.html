33. What is the posiblle relation between the sender and the recipient


The possible relation between the sender and the recipient can vary depending on the context of the communication. Here are some common possibilities:

1. Friends: The sender and recipient could be friends who have a personal relationship. They may communicate casually, share personal experiences, or discuss common interests.

2. Family Members: The sender and recipient could be family members, such as siblings, parents and children, or extended relatives. The communication may involve family matters, updates, or emotional support.

3. Colleagues: The sender and recipient could be colleagues or coworkers within a professional setting. They may communicate regarding work-related matters, projects, updates, or collaboration.

4. Acquaintances: The sender and recipient could be acquaintances who have a casual or superficial relationship. They may communicate occasionally, share general information, or engage in light conversations.

5. Romantic Partners: The sender and recipient could be romantic partners or individuals in a romantic relationship. Their communication may involve expressions of love, emotional support, future plans, or intimate discussions.

6. Service Providers and Customers: The sender and recipient could have a professional relationship where the sender provides a service or product to the recipient as a customer. The communication may be related to inquiries, orders, or customer service interactions.

7. Teacher and Student: The sender and recipient could be a teacher and a student. The communication may involve instructions, guidance, feedback, or academic-related discussions.

These are just a few examples, and there can be various other relationships between the sender and the recipient depending on the specific context of the communication.

36. give the right scema of right sentences from cause and effen relation


Cause and Effect

Give the right scheme of cause and effect sentences

Cause and Effect Relation

Cause secara sederhana diartikan sebagai alasan atau motif seseorang melakukan sebuah tindakan. Dalam kata lain, cause adalah alasan mengapa sesuatu terjadi. Effect diartikan hasil atau akibat dari sebuah tindakan.

Pembahasan

Contoh scheme of cause and effect sentences:

S + V  + due to + Noun

I couldn't go anywhere due to a lot of homework and tests.

Cause = a lot of homework and tests

Effect = I couldn't go anywhere.

Terjemahannya

Saya tidak bisa kemana-mana karena banyak pekerjaan rumah dan ujian.

Penyebab = banyak pekerjaan rumah dan tes

Efek = Saya tidak bisa pergi ke mana pun.

S + V + because + S + V

Arfan got wet because it rained so hard.

Cause = It rained so hard.  

Effect = Arfan got wet.

Terjemahannya

Arfan menjadi basah karena hujan sangat deras.

Penyebab = Hujan sangat deras.

Efek = Arfan menjadi basah.

Dalam Bahasa Inggris untuk menyatakan cause and effect digunakan signal words sebagai berikut:

due to

as a result of

because of                              

as a consequence of                       +                  Noun

owing to

thanks to  

because

since                                               +                  S + Verb

as

for
