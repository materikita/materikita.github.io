34. Fiona, anuar and reza had some cards. Fiona and anuar had a total of 349. Fiona had 145 cards. Anuar had 3 times as many cards as reza. How many cards did fiona and reza have altogether?


Jawaban:

213 cards

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Fiona + Anuar = 349

Anuar = 349- 145 = 204

3 units = 204

1 unit = 204 ÷ 3 = 68

Reza have 68 cards

altogether = fiona + reza

= 145 + 68

= 213cards
