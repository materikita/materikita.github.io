31. all skin contains a coloring substance as a protection from burning by the sun.This substance called


It is called melanin. Melanin is pigment produced by melanocytes, which are found in the stratum basale of the epidermis (outer layer of the skin). Although human beings generally have a similar concentration of melanocytes in their skin, the melanocytes in some individuals and races more frequently or less frequently express the melanin-producing genes, thereby resulting a greater or lesser concentration of skin melanin.
