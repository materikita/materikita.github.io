31. Write negative form1. I (not cry)2. You (not lie)3. We (not work)4. Ali (not sleep)5. She (not eat)​


Jawaban:

1. I do not cry

2. You do not lie

3. We do not work

4. Ali does not sleep

5. She does not eat

Penjelasan:

Because rumus dari :

NEGATIVE FORM (-) :  

Subject ( I, You, We, They ) + do not / don’t + V1  ( First Form of Verb )

Subject ( He, She, It ) + does not / doesn’t + V1  ( First Form of Verb )

Jawaban:

1. I (not cry)

*I (do not cry).

2. You (not lie)

*You (do not lie).

3. We (not work)

*We (do not work).

4. Ali (not sleep)

*Ali (does not sleep).

5. She (not eat)​

*She (does not eat).

Penjelasan:

Simple Present Tense is the tense (sentence pattern) used to express habits (habitual action), general truths (facts), or events that occur at this time.

37. you didn`t eat my toast (?) (+)​


Jawaban

(-) You didn't eat my toast.

(+) You ate my toast.

(?) Did you eat my toast?

Penjelasan

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian masa lampau.

Formula:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Berikut contohnya:

(+) She played chess last night.

(-) She did not play chess last night.

(?) Did she play chess last night?

(+) They went to a party.

(-) They did not go to a party.

(?) Did they go to a party?

(+) He broke up with his girlfriend.

(-) He did not brake up with his girlfriend.

(?) Did he brake up with his girlfriend?
