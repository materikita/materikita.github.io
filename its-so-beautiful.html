32. Choose the right pronoun.1 ) The flowers in my garden so beautiful. My grandma waters (it/they/them/its) every morning.2 ) My father has many tools. (It/They/Its/Their) are realy functional.help me...​


Jawaban:

1. It

2. They

Penjelasan:

1. Karena menggunakan simple present tense :

My grandma   waters     it        every morning

         S       +     V1(-s) +  O      +      Adverb

2. They digunakan sebagai subject pengganti many tools yang berbentuk jamak. Are realy functional merujuk pada kegunaan many tools. Jadi many tools yang seharusnya menjadi subject bisa diganti dengan they.
