Banyaknya permutasi dari kata "MILENIAL" dengan huruf L tidak saling berdampingan adalah [tex]\boxed{56\:susunan\:kata}[/tex]

Pendahuluan:

Susunan kata atau yang disebut dengan permutasi ialah sebuah bentuk penyusunan dalam ilmu matematika yang dimana konsep nya ialah susunan yang berbeda dari awal. Rumus permutasi umum yakni:

[tex]\boxed{P=\frac{N!}{K!}}[/tex]

Yang dimana:

P merupakan permutasi.N! ialah jumlah seluruh angka dari objek.K! merupakan jumlah dari objek yang dipilih.

Berikut rumus permutasi yang lain:

Rumus permutasi (memperhatikan ukuran) yakni: [tex]\boxed{P=\frac{n!}{(n-r)!}}[/tex]Rumus permutasi "n" unsur yakni: [tex]\boxed{P_n=n!}[/tex]Rumus permutasi "n" unsur sama yakni: [tex]\boxed{P=\frac{n!}{k!l!m!}}[/tex]Rumus permutasi siklis yakni: [tex]\boxed{P_s=(n-1)!}[/tex]Pembahasan:

Diketahui:

Milenial.Huruf L tidak saling berdampingan.

Ditanya:

Tentukan permutasi nya!

Jawab:

[tex]M+i+l+e+n+a[/tex]

[tex]= 1+2+2+1+1+1[/tex]

[tex]=8[/tex]

[tex]\frac{8!}{6!}[/tex]

[tex]=\frac{8\:.\: 7 .\: 6 \:.\: 5 \:.\: 4 \:.\: 3\:.\: 2 \:.\: 1}{6 \:.\: 5 \:.\: 4 \:.\: 3 \:.\: 2 \:.\: 1}[/tex]

[tex]=\frac{40320}{720}[/tex]

[tex]=\boxed{56\:susunan\:kata}[/tex]

Jadi, permutasi dari kata milenial dengan huruf L yang tidak saling berdampingan ialah [tex]\boxed{56\:susunan\:kata}[/tex]

Pembahasan Past Perfect

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg sudah selesai di waktu lampau pada Kegiatan lain di masa lalu juga.

Rumus Past Perfect

1. Verbal sentence

(+) S + had + V3 (-) S + had not + V3(?) Had + S + V3

2. Nominal sentence

(+) S + had + been + complement (-) S + had not + been + complement (?) had S + been + complement?

Catatan

Never = not ever

Jadi kalimat Positive dan Interrogative menggunakan everSedangkan Negative menggunakan not ever atau never

Perintah Soal bagian pertama:

Answer the questions below by using past perfect tense, then change into (+), (-) or (?) sentence.

[Jawab pertanyaan-pertanyaan di bawah ini dengan menggunakan past perfect tense, lalu ubah menjadi kalimat (+), (-) atau (?).]

1. Never I... such delicious pies before visiting the bakery. (Taste)

(+) I had ever tasted such delicious pies before visiting the bakery(-) I had never tasted such delicious pies before visiting the bakery(?) Had I ever tasted such delicious pies before visiting the bakery?

2. He told that he ... all the files in the directory. (remove)

(+) He told that he had removed all the files in the directory.(-) He told that he had not removed all the files in the directory.(?) He told that had he removed all the files in the directory?

3. The student ... her disposable lunch box in the garbage can by the time the bell rang. (drop)

(+) The student had dropped her disposable lunch box in the garbage can by the time the bell rang.(-) The student had not dropped her disposable lunch box in the garbage can by the time the bell rang.(?) had The student dropped her disposable lunch box in the garbage can by the time the bell rang?

4. When he arrived at the station, the train...(leave)

(+) When he arrived at the station, the train had left(-) When he arrived at the station, the train had not left(?) When he arrived at the station, HAD The train left?

5. ... the room .. brighter before the participants came? (be)

(+) the room had been brighter before the participants came(-) the room had not been brighter before the participants came(?) had the room been brighter before the participants came?

6. If I a place, I would have spread the good news to everyone. (offer)

(+) If I had a place, I would have spread the good news to everyone.(-) If I had not a place, I would have spread the good news to everyone.(?) If I had a place, WOULD I have spread the good news to everyone?

Perintah Soal bagian kedua;

Chance these sentences into past perfect tense by using English.

[Ubah kalimat ini menjadi past perfect tense dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris.]

7. Semua siswa telah belajar sebelum guru mereka memberikan kuis.

=> All students had studied before their teacher gave the quiz.

8. Ayahku belum datang ketika Ibuku pergi ke pasar.

=> My dad had not been here when my mom went to the market.

9. Tria mandi setelah dia main basket

=> Tria took a shower after she had played basketball

CATATAN

Jika memang harus diubah ke kalimat Positive Berarti No. 10 menjadi;
