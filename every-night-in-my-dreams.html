9. My sister ………(not call) me every night from Bogor


Simple present, ditandai dengan 'every night'.

Pattern :

(+) S + V1 + s/es + O

(-) S + do/does + V1 + O

(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O?


=> My sister does not call me every night from Bogor.


Note :

• Jika subjek berupa she, he, it (tunggal) maka menggunakan V1+s/es atau does.

20. 3.(+) My Sister Calls My Mother Every Night(-)...(?)...ubah ke bentuk kalimat negatif & introgatif.


Dalam Hal ini Menggunakan Simple Present Tense

(+) My Sister Calls My Mother Every Night

(-) My Sister Doesn't Call My mother
Every Night

(?) Does My Sister Call My Mother Every Night.


Jawaban:

(+) My Sister Calls My Mother Every Night

(-) My sister does not call my mother every night

(?)does my sister call my mother every night?

Penjelasan:

PRESENT TENSE

(+)Subject+Verb1+Object/Complement

jika orang ketiga tunggal  yaitu he,she,it maka verb nya ditambahkan s /es

example : she reads, he watches

(-)Subject+do/does+not+verb1+object/complement

does->she,he,it

do->i,you,we,they

bila ada does maka verb nya tidak ditambahkan s/es karena sudah ada DOES

(?)do/does+subject+verb1+object/complement+?

28. I&my friend watch tv every night (Ubah ke passive) + - ?


My friend and I watch tv every night.


Ubah ke bentuk passive, diatas merupakan simple present, maka rumus passivenya adalah :

(+) S + is/am/are + V3 + by + O

(-) S + is/am/are + V3 + by + O

(?) is/am/are + S + V3 + by + O?


=> (+) TV are watched by my friend and me every night.

=> (-) TV are not watched by my friend and me every night.

=> (?) Are TV watched by my friend and me?


Note :

•-• subjek dikalimat pasif menjadi objek dikalimat aktif.

•-• objek dikalimat aktif menjadi subjek dikalimat pasif.

•-• am jika subjek I

•-• is jika subjek he,she, it (singular)

•-• are jika subjek they, we, you, it (plural).
