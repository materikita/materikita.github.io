1. I did not know the way to my hotel … I asked a porter


Jawaban :

Penjelasan :

Penggunaan so dalam sebuah kalimat bisa diikuti oelh sebuah kata sifat (adjv), adverb maupun non phrase yang akan diawali oleh kata much, few dan little.

RUMUS :

So + Adj/Adv/Noun Phrase

Contoh kalimat penggunaan so :

My little sister dances so beautifully Rina is so creative and innovativeThere were so many people came to her birthday party This new car is so fast This litte baby cries so loudly The food was so delicious My grandfather has so many old cars
