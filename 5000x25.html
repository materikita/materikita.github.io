50 + 80 + 50 = 180 Are.

Jawaban = 180 Are.

= 0,5 hm² + 80 dam² + 5000 m² = ... are

maka,

0,5 hm² = 0,5 × 100 = 50 are

80 dam² = 80 × 1 = 80 are

5000 m² = 5000 ÷ 100 = 50 are

jadi,

= 50 are + 80 are + 50 are

= 100 are + 80 are

= 180 are ✓
