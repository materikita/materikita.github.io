6. Arjuna studies english in his room every sunday afternoon(negative and interrogative)​


Jawab :

(+) Arjuna studies English in his room every sunday afternoon.
(-) Arjuna does not study English in his room every sunday afternoon.
(?) Does Arjuna study English in his room every sunday afternoon?

Pembahasan :
Tenses yang digunakan adalah simple present tense., yaitu tenses yang menunjukkan kebiasaan atau aktivitas sehari-hari. Pada kalimat diatas ditandai dengan 'every sunday afternoon'. Pattern :
(+) S + V1-s/es + O.
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O.
(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O?

catatan:
• tambahan s/es hanya digunakan jika subjek berupa she, he, it, nama orang (singular).
