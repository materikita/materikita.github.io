Hello, my name is Anastasyra You can call her by her short name, namely Asyra. (guise)

I come from Jember, and was born on November 3, 2008.

I had a romantic experience last year :) Yes, where there is a romantic word there must be love.

I think it's the best experience for more than 12 years, I always remember it when I miss him.

Call it R, let's hear my story:

One day when I was in 6th grade, my friends and I in my group were working on making paper flowers.

Briefly, a few moments I saw R take flowers from one of my friends. I thought he was going to make fun of me, for not having a good physique.

But instead, he approached and gave the flower shamelessly in front of everyone. Since I'm still innocent, I don't understand the meaning.

Some of my friends cheered like a crowd cheering on a dancing couple. I felt embarrassed, and from then on I hated him.

Long story short, in the year of covid (2021) at the beginning of that year somehow my innocence disappeared

Love began to emerge when we were almost separated, maybe fate said otherwise he was also in the same school with me.

Shame, anger, and love are still mixed in my heart. Every time I see R, I don't dare to look him in the eyes but turn my head down and show shame.

That's all from my story, I hope you take lessons from true stories. Hope you enjoy :)
