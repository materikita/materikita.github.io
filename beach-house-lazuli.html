1. batu pirus lazuli merupakan gabungan antara baku ... dan ...​


Jawaban:

gabungan dari pirus dan lazuli

Penjelasan:

Batu pirus dengan nama lain Turquoise merupakan jenis batu cincin yang oleh masyarakat Indonesia dan dunia sudah dikenal sejak lama. Tahukah anda masyarakat mempercayai bahwa pirus ini sebagai batu berasal dari kepulauan Arab, lebih tepatnya lagi diwilayah Persia sekarang Republik Islam Iran, yang merupakan penghasil pirus dengan kualitas terbaik, batu jenis ini juga banyak didapatkan dari penambangan di wilayah Nishapur di negara Iran dengan kualitas batu yang cukup bagus pula.

Sekerang baru-baru ini ada batu namanya "Pirus Lazuli" ini tiada lain adalah nama gabungan dari pirus dan lazuli (lapis lazuli) karena dalam batu ini memuat karakter yang dimiliki batu pirus yang terpadu dengan karakter yang dimiliki oleh batu lazuli. Batu ini ditemukan di daerah Kab. Tulungagung Kec. Pucanglaban.

31. description nambo beach and itu conclusion nambo beach ​


Nambo Beach is a mainstay tourist attraction in Southeast Sulawesi Province. This beach is located in Abeli District, approximately 12 kilometers from the center of Kendari City. This fine white sandy beach along the shoreline with sloping conditions has become one of the favorite tourist attractions in Kendari City which is visited by many tourists. Not only local tourists, often foreign tourists also stop by and enjoy the beauty of this beach.

Nambo Beach has waves that are not too big, so tourists can swim around the beach. In addition to swimming, tourists can also play in the sand on the beach, take a walk along the beach, sunbathe on the sand while enjoying the breeze that blows and joking with friends or family.

Another attraction of this tourist attraction is the presence of palm trees that grow along the coast. These trees are often used as shelter from the hot sun. In addition to being useful as a shelter, this palm tree by local residents is also used to increase their income, by selling the fruit from the palm tree.

The existing facilities at this tourist attraction are quite adequate, such as the many inns with varying prices, food stalls, public toilets, gazebos that stand along the beach, souvenir shops and a large parking lot.

To go to this tourist attraction if from Kendari City can be reached by using a motorized vehicle if by land or using a fishing boat if by sea. Travel time to get to this attraction is about 15 minutes.

conclusion:

Nambo Beach is a mainstay tourist attraction in Southeast Sulawesi Province. This beach is located in Abeli District, approximately 12 kilometers from the center of Kendari City. This fine white sandy beach along the shoreline with sloping conditions has become one of the favorite tourist attractions in Kendari City which is visited by many tourists. Not only local tourists, often foreign tourists also stop by and enjoy the beauty of this beach.

37. They (have) (has) a beautiful house near the beach


Jawaban:

They have a beautiful house near the beach.

Supaya lain kali gak bingung lagi tentang penggunan have sama has, nih cara bedainnya.

-Penggunaan have selalu dipasangkan dengan I, we, you, they, dan subjek    lainya yang jamak.

-Penggunan has digunakan untuk subjek seperti she, he, it, dan subjek  lainnya yang tunggal.

They have a beautiful house near the beach

Catatan :

Untuk subjek I you they we pake kata kerja have (bentuk present)

Untuk subjek he she it pake kata kerja diiakhiri s/es, tp khusus untuk kata kerja have jadi has.
