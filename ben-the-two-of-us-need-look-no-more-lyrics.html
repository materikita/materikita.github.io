11. a number contains of two digits. the ones is two more than the tenth. if the sum of the ones and the tenth is six times the differences, then the number is


Jawab:

The number is 75

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Misalnya :

puluhan = x dan satuan = y

maka menjadi xy

x = y + 2.....(1)

x+y = 6(x-y).....(2)

x+y = 6x - 6y

y + 6y = 6x - x

7y = 5x

y = 5/7x

disubstitusi ke pers 1

x = y + 2

x = 5/7x + 2

x - 5/7x = 2

2/7x = 2

x = 2 . 7/2

x = 7

disubstitusi ke pers 2

y = 5/7 x

y = 5/7 (7)

y = 5

The number is 75
