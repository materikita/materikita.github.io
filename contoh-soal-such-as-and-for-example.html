3. Soal pilihan ganda tentang for example dan such as


Explanation:

For example can be used both within a sentence and at the start of a sentence.

Example:

- I like to eat sweet food, for example, jelly, ice cream, and chocolate bar. (within a sentence)

- I learnt some new things in the camp yesterday. For example, I made a fire with sticks and tie various knots. (at the start of a sentence)

Please note that in written, For example always has a comma after it. Moreover, If it is used within a sentence, it will be followed by noun or noun phrase and if it is used at the start of a sentence, it will be followed by a full sentence.

 

Such as can be used within a sentence and in the middle of a sentence.

Example:

- I can speak a few foreign languages, such as English, Japanese, and Chinese. (within a sentence)

- Sportswear brands such as nike and adidas manufacture their products in many different countries around the world. (in the middle of a sentence)

Please note that in written, such as does not have a comma after it. Moreover, such as in the middle of a sentence does not have comma before and after it.

6. HELPP...1. What is the difference between 'for example' and 'such as'?2. When do we use 'for example'?


Use For example, at the start of a sentence.
- Use such as in the middle of a sentence.

Compare these examples:

Children should eat less junk food. For example,they should avoid eating burgers and chips.

Children should avoid eating junk food such asburgers and chips.

Notice that 'For example' is followed by a comma and a full sentence. 'Such as' is in the middle of the sentence, followed by two nouns. You can use 'For instance' instead of 'For example'. You can use 'like' instead of 'such as'

9. bagaimana cara penggunaan dari "for example" dan "such as" ?mohon bantuannya...


For Example, For Instance, Such as, E.g

Ada sedikit perbedaan antara "For Example" dan "For Instance". Keduanya memiliki arti yang sama, dan keduanya digunakan dengan cara yang sama. Kedua ungkapan ini digunakan ketika pembicara atau penulis ingin memperkenalkan orang atau hal tertentu yang membantu menjelaskan atau mengkonfirmasi pernyataan umum. Perhatikan, bagaimanapun "For Example" digunakan lebih sering daripada "For Instance", khususnya dalam konteks formal, jadi dalam penulisan akademis "For Example" adalah pilihan yang lebih aman.

Beberapa kalimat berikut di bawah ini adalah contoh contoh kalimat dengan menggunakan kata for example dan for instance.

The situation is slowly improving. Last month, for example, the company achieved record sales in Europe.  (Situasi perlahan membaik. Bulan lalu, misalnya, perusahaan mencapai rekor penjualan di Eropa.)It was obvious that her memory was failing. For example, she would often forget where she put her car keys.  (Sudah jelas ingatannya buruk. Misalnya, dia sering lupa di mana dia meletakkan kunci mobilnya.)A lot of my friends were there—John and Linda, for example.  (Banyak teman saya ada di sana — John dan Linda, misalnya.)Poor balance is a problem for a lot of older people, like my grandmother, for instance.  (Keseimbangan yang buruk adalah masalah bagi banyak orang tua, seperti nenek saya, misalnya.)Mobile devices are becoming less expensive. Sophisticated smartphones, for instance, can now be purchased for less than $ 100.  (Perangkat seluler menjadi lebih murah. Smartphone canggih, misalnya, sekarang dapat dibeli dengan harga kurang dari $ 100.)Other Sentences

For Example

The situation is slowly improving. Last month, for example, the company achieved record sales in Europe.  (Situasi perlahan membaik. Bulan lalu, misalnya, perusahaan mencapai rekor penjualan di Eropa.)It was obvious that her memory was failing. For example, she would often forget where she put her car keys.  (Sudah jelas ingatannya buruk. Misalnya, dia sering lupa di mana dia meletakkan kunci mobilnya.)A lot of my friends were there—John and Linda, for example.  (Banyak teman saya ada di sana — John dan Linda, misalnya.)

In this forest, you’ll see many types of coniferous trees, such as pine and spruce. 14. apa perbedaan 'such as' dan 'for example'? dan berikan contohnya!


Jawaban:

The difference between 'such as' and 'for example', which actually has the same meaning, lies on the position in the sentence. Here is the example:

Several notable football managers begin to lose the opportunity to lead big teams. For example, Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez can only be in the non elite teams this season.Several notable football managers such as Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez begin to lose the opportunity to lead big teams.

Penjelasan:

The phrase 'for example' and 'such as' have the same meaning, that is to state the examples related to the content in the sentence. Yet, they are quite different in terms of their position in a sentence.

'For example' can be used in the middle of the sentence as well as at the beginning of it. Moreover, it has to be between two commas when it is put in the middle of the sentence. Here is how: The students have less interest in social science, for example, history, geography, sociology. The students have less interest in social science. For example, they tend to be lazy in learning history or geography.

'Such as' can only be used in the middle of the sentence but not at the beginning. Yet, this phrase does not need two commas in between. Here is how:                                      

Several notable football manager such as Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez begin to lose the opportunity to lead elite teams

33. buat contoh kalimat for example and for instance :isi dimana letak komabuat contoh such as and like:isi dimana letak koma


Jawaban:

1. For example adalah kata yang sering digunakan untuk menujukkan contoh, namun ada beberapa hal yang perlu diperhatikan yaitu:

A. Jika for example terletak di awal kalimat, harus diikuti dengan koma:

There are many activities in the morning. For example, the student goes to school.

B. Jika for example terletak di tengah kalimat, umumnya antara subjek dan predikat :

Social security, for example, is the responsibility of our government.

2. For instance memiliki fungsi yang sama dengan for example. Namun, umumnya for instance lebih sering digunakan di tengah kalimat.

In the economic sector, for instance, Indonesia is still better than Thailand.

3. e.g. juga dapat digunakan untuk memberikan contoh. E.g. sendiri adalah kependekan dari exempli gratia yang merupakan berasal dari bahasa Latin.

High level smartphones, (e.g. Samsung and Iphone) are the leader of the world market

Today people, e.g. children, play with smartphone.

39. Buatlah dialog 2 orang tentang 'for example' dan 'such as' masing2 lima kalimat? *tolong yakk:))*


Kita sering kali ingin memberikan contoh ketika menggunakan bahasa Inggris. Phrase yang dapat kita gunakan adalah for example atau such as. Kedua phrase ini fungsinya sama tapi dalam cara penggunaannya berbeda.

Pembahasan

Fungsi dari such as dan for example hampir sama, yaitu digunakan untuk menyebutkan contoh. Misalnya ketika sedang membicarakan suatu topik tertentu dan kita ingin memberikan contoh dari topik tersebut. Perhatikan contoh dibawah ini:

We should eat more healthy food, for example salad, fruit, and vegetables..You should do more exercise to make your body healthier, such as jogging, playing football, and swimming.Perbedaan Such as dan For Example

Dalam beberapa kasus terdapat perbedaan dalam penggunaan such as dan for example. Berikut ini perbedaan cara penggunaan such as dan for example:

For example dapat digunakan untuk mengawali kalimat sedangkan such as tidak.For example tidak dapat memotong sebuah klausa sedangkan such as bisa.Dialog Bahasa Inggris Menggunakan Such As dan For Example

Berikut ini adalah contoh dialog bahasa Inggris yang terdapat penggunaan such as dan for example:

Jane: Hai Tom. I have a problem with my body.

Tom: What is it?

Jane: I can lose my weight easily. Do you have any suggestions?

Tom. I think you should stop eating junk food, such as hot dogs, hamburgers, and pizza.

Jane: Yeah. It is very difficult to do that because I like hot dogs and numbers very much.

Tom: You should do you best if you want to lose you weight fast. Then you have to do more exercise, for example playing volleyball, running, and swimming.

Jane: I will try to do more exercise then.

Tom: That isn't enough. You have to start to eat more healthy food, such as vegetables and fruit.

Jane: I see. Okay. I’ll follow your suggestion. Thank you, Tom.

Tom: Anytime, Jane.
