Jawaban:

Boyzone – Everyday I Love You

Adalah sepasang kekasih yang baru membina sebuah rumah tangga, mereka berkomitmen untuk mengatakan “I Love You” setiap kali mereka akan berpisah. Setiap hari Si Pria pergi ke kantor dan dan Sang Wanita tinggal di rumah mengurus rumah. Itu artinya setiap hari mereka mengucapkan “I Love You” satu sama lain. Suatu hari mereka bertengkar dan tidak saling mengucapkan “I Love You” lagi. Suatu hari pula saat Si Pria berangkat ke kantor, sebelum meninggalkan pintu rumah, tiba-tiba Si Wanita mengucapkan “I Love You” pada suaminya tapi suaminya tidak membalasnya. Beberapa jam kemudian Si Wanita mendapat kabar kalau suaminya mengalami kecelakaan dan telah meninggal. Tapi Si Wanita tidak menyesali apa yang telah terjadi karena sebelumnya ia masih sempat mengucapkan “I Love You” pada suaminya sebelum hal yang terakhir yang bisa lakukan di dunia ini, kematian. Dan bahwa setiap hari mereka sebenarnya saling mencintai. Ini cuma cerita, sebaiknya dengarin Everyday I Love You-nya Boyzone aja. 37. When you read a biography article of a famous person, you will likely find that the article includes pictures of the person, like Cristiano Ronaldo’s picture that you see before you start reading. Think about these questions and discuss them in pairs. - What do you notice first from the picture? - What does the picture tell you? - How does the picture relate to the written text? - What happens with the message of the text if the picture is deleted?​


What do you notice first from the picture?

When looking at a picture accompanying a biography article, the first thing that catches the eye is often the person's face or their prominent features. In the case of Cristiano Ronaldo, for example, his face, expression, and possibly his distinctive hairstyle might stand out.

What does the picture tell you?

Pictures provide a visual representation of the person being discussed. They offer insights into the subject's appearance, age, style, and sometimes even their personality. In the case of a famous athlete like Cristiano Ronaldo, his picture might convey his athleticism, fame, and possibly his confidence or intensity.

How does the picture relate to the written text?

The picture complements the written text by providing a visual context. It helps readers connect the words with the actual person, making the biography more relatable and engaging. For instance, if the text describes Ronaldo's achievements, the picture of him celebrating a goal might emphasize his success on the field.

What happens with the message of the text if the picture is deleted?

Without the picture, the text might lose some of its personal and relatable elements. Descriptions in the text would remain, but the reader's ability to visualize the subject would be diminished. The emotional or visual impact of the person's accomplishments, struggles, or personality traits might be lessened without the visual aid.

Pembahasan

Kesimpulannya, gambar dalam artikel biografi memainkan peran penting dalam meningkatkan hubungan pembaca dengan subjek dan memperdalam pemahaman mereka. Mereka memberikan titik masuk visual ke dalam kehidupan dan pencapaian seseorang, dan ketidakhadiran mereka berpotensi melemahkan dampak teks tertulis. Kombinasi teks dan gambar menciptakan gambaran cerita individu yang lebih komprehensif dan menarik.
