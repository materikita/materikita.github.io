8. If you could have any superpower, which one would you want and why ?


If you could have any superpower, which one would you want why?

Kalimat tersebut menanyakan tentang pengandaian atas hal yang mustahil terjadi. Sehingga soal tersebut harus dijawab dengan conditional sentence type 2. Rumusnya yaitu:

If + simple past, present conditionalPresent conditional + if + simple past

Sehingga kalimat untuk menjawab pertanyaan diatas adalah If i could have any superpower, i would choose time travel. If i can do time travel, i would go to the past and fix all the embarassing moment i had in my life.

Pembahasan

Conditional sentence tipe 2 digunakan untuk mengungkapkan sesuatu yang tidak realistis atau sangat kecil kemungkinannya terjadi. Karena itu tipe ini digunakan untuk menyatakan angan-angan atau hal mustahil lainnya.

Rumus dari kalimat tersebut yaitu:

If + simple past, present conditionalPresent conditional + if + simple past

How to Ask for Things in EnglishJawaban
Pendahuluan

Bertanya atau menanyakan tentang sesuatu dalam bahasa Inggris tidak ada patokan yang pasti karena bervariasi tergantung dari konteks pertanyaan, situasi, pertanyaan yang ditujukan pada siapa, dll. Berikut ini akan kakak jelaskan sedikit tentang beberapa situasi yang mungkin terjadi.


Pembahasan

Menanyakan kepada layanan bantuan/informasi

Hello, Can/Could I have …….. please?Good morning, can/could you give/get me …….. please?Good evening, A table for two, please.

Interupsi orang lain untuk bertanya tentang sesuatu:

.... Do you know if...? .... Do you have...? .... Do you accept .... (credit cards)? .... Is this the right way for.... (the Post Office)? .... Could you tell me if .... (there's a Post Office near here)?

Dalam situasi yang formal

.... Would you mind .... (keeping an eye on my luggage?).... I wonder if you could .... (move your suitcase a little.)Kesimpulan

Ketika kamu menanyakan tentang sesuatu atau seseorang, tanyalah dengan sopan. Berikut ini adalah beberapa cara untuk terdengar lebih sopan.

1. Memberikan ucapan greeting seperti hello, good morning, dll.

2. Jangan lupakan kata please dan thank you.

3. Ucapkan excuse me.

40. Do you need any help? (my) (want) (some)


Jawaban:

I would describe the difference as indefinite (any) vs definite (some) i.e.

Do you need any help? = compare this with indefinite: Do you need a pencil? (i.e. something to write with - not one particular pencil)

Do you need some help? = compare this with definite: Do you need the pencil? (i.e. a particular pencil - e.g. the blue one next to my book)

We use definite words to talk about things which both the speaker and the listener know about and which usually are in the same environment e.g.

An old lady cannot lift her bag onto the train. A young man says Do you need some help? - the old lady knows exactly what help he means (i.e. to lift the bag for her).

He could also correctly say Do you need any help? but in this case it is less certain how he might help her.

Penjelasan:

I heard people saying do you need any help ? but i thought the correct way of saying it was do you need some help ?

Also I heard the normal usage of "some" and "any" in questions is

- If the questioner is expecting the answer to be Yes, she will use "some".

- If the questioner has no expectations about whether the answer will be Yes or No, she will use "any".

so the answer:

My:Do you need my help?

Want:do you want any help?

some:do you need some help ?
