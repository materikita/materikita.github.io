Wrap me up in all your = make me yoursI want you in my arms = I want to hug youI was lost within the darkness = The writer of the lyrics was felt like he was trapped in difficult situation that makes him think that he is lost and can’t escape from it.Georgia, pulled me in = Georgia was attracting the writer and the writer want to know about Georgia moreI would never fall unless it’s you I fall into = the writer just want to be in love with her and it express how the writer how much he love her.

metaphor, simile, personification, hyperbole and symbolism.Lagu Until I Found You oleh Stephen Sanchez adalah lagu yang ditulis oleh Stephen untuk kekasihnya. 31. If you (not believe) what I say, ask your mother.

*If you (don't believe) what I say, ask your mother.

32. If he (like) the house, will he buy it?

*If he (likes) the house, will he buy it?

33. If you will kindly sit down, I (make) enquiries for you.

*If you will kindly sit down, I (will make) enquiries for you.

*Unless I have a quiet room I (won't be able) to do anything work.

35. She won’t open the door unless she (know) who it is.

*She won't open the door unless she (knows) who it is.

Penjelasan:

Conditional Sentences type 1 is used to describe situations that may occur in the future. In the type 1 conditional sentences (if clause) is in the form of Simple Present and the main sentence is in the form of Simple Future.
