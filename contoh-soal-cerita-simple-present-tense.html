1. Present tense
Pattern :
Untuk kalimat verbal (dengan kata kerja) :
S + V +s/es + o/c. (+)
S + do/does + not + V + o/c.
ex :
I go to school every day by bus.
He plays football with his friend every sunday. 
They don't come to course.

Untuk kalimat Nominal (dengan kata benda, kata sifat)
S + to be + noun/adj (+)
S + to be + not + noun/adj (-)
ex :
She is so cute.
I am a student.
They are not a good friend.

Present Continous
S +to be + Ving + o/c. (+)
S + tobe + not + Ving + o/c.
Ex
I am visiting market now.
He is playing football with his friend. 
They are coming to my house.

16. contoh cerita diri sendiri tentang simple present tense minimal 250 kata


Simple Present Tense adalah jenis kalimat dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan kegiatan yang berulang, kebiasaan, atau pengetahuan awam (yang hampir semua orang tahu).

Contoh kegiatan yang berulang: I eat breakfast every morning.

Contoh kebiasaan: I usually watch TV 3 hours everyday.

Contoh pengetahuan awam: The Earth circles the sun.


Formulasi Simple Present Tense

Kata kerja yang digunakan adalah Verb 1 (base form of the verb). Untuk pertanyaan, kata "do" dipakai, dan untuk kalimat negatif, kata "do not/don't" dipakai.

Untuk kata ganti orang ketiga (dia; he, she, it), kata "does" dan does not/doesn't" lah yang dipakai.

Contoh:

You speak English.

You don't speak English.

Do you speak English?

She speaks English.

She doesn't speak English.

Does she speak English?

Cerita tentang diri sendiri dalam Simple Present Tense pada dasarnya kamu menceritakan tentang diri kamu: nama, umur, hobi, tempat tinggal, kegiatan sehari-hari, dll.

Contohnya seperti ini:

My name is Vinia. I am 30 years old. I work from Monday to Friday, from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. I usually drive my car to work.

I live in Pennsylvania, USA. I live with my husband. I have a dog. My hobbies are singing, watching movies, listening to music, and reading books.
