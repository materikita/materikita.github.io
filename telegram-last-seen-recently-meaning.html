25. I haven't seen neither of them since last week apakah penempatan kata neither tersebut sudah benar?


Answer:

The sentence ' I haven't seen neither of them since last week. ' is I'm afraid mistaken despite the correct position of 'neither' in the sentence.
'Neither' is used with positive sentence as 'neither' has already had the meaning of 'not for both parties/things'. If you want to use negative sentence like, use 'Either' instead. So, when you state such condition in a sentence like above, the right one should be like these:
- I haven't seen either of them since last week.'
OR
- I have seen neither of them since last week.

Hopefully it's clear enough for you.

Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori: similarities and difference
