9. Steven sold 94 more sticks of satay than Vanessa, but 59 fewer sticks of satay than Alex. Three of them sold 691 sticks of satay altogether. How many sticks of satay did Alex sell?


Let's assume that the number of satay sticks sold by Vanessa is V.

According to the given information, Steven sold 94 more sticks of satay than Vanessa, so the number of satay sticks sold by Steven would be V + 94.

Also, Steven sold 59 fewer sticks of satay than Alex, so the number of satay sticks sold by Alex would be (V + 94) + 59.

Together, the three of them sold 691 sticks of satay, so we can set up the equation:

V + (V + 94) + ((V + 94) + 59) = 691

Simplifying the equation:

3V + 247 = 691

Subtracting 247 from both sides:

3V = 691 - 247

3V = 444

Dividing both sides by 3:

V = 444 / 3

V = 148

Therefore, Vanessa sold 148 sticks of satay.

To find out how many sticks of satay Alex sold, we substitute V into the equation for Alex's sales:

Alex = (V + 94) + 59

Alex = (148 + 94) + 59

Alex = 242 + 59

Alex = 301

Therefore, Alex sold 301 sticks of satay.

24. my brother made that pough diganti passive voice


Karena kalimat di atas bentuk SIMPLE PAST, dimana setelah subject "my brother" menggunakan verb ke-2 dari "make" yaitu "MADE" maka rumus passive nya adalah :

Subject + to be (was/were) + V3 + by...

Catatan !!!
Ingat, subjek dikalimat aktif berubah jadi objek di kalimat pasif, begitu pula sebaliknya.

Jadi, jawabannya adalah :
Active : My brother made that pough.

Passive : That pough WAS MADE by my brother.

===============================

Thunderstorms can occur because there is the potential difference between cloud and ground or cloud to cloud a different charge. If the potential difference between the cloud and the earth is large, then disposal of electrons occurs in order to be balanced. Electrons are discharged through the air. When the electrons can penetrate the insulating air boundary then an explosion of sound.
