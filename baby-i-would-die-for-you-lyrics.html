9. -if you had to change the lyrics would you change?why? Song: "I believe i can fly"Mohon bantuannya kak:)​


Jawaban:

Notes for the Teacher

1. The song

Do a search on the Internet to find the song “I Believe I Can Fly” by R. Kelly. When you

search, be sure to include the title and the name of the artist.

2. Song background

R. Kelly is an R & B (rhythm and blues) singer and songwriter. He first sang in subway

stations in Chicago, Illinois, but now he is very famous. In fact, he was one of the most

successful R & B performers of the 1990s.

R. Kelly first sang this song, “I Believe I Can Fly,” in 1996. R. Kelly says that he wrote

the song for Michael Jordon, a very famous basketball player who jumps very high. The

song is inspirational -- it tries to help people succeed in life.

3. Grammar background

This song uses the modal verb can to expresses ability.

Write these examples on the board. Point out to students that can is followed by the

simple form of the verb.

A bird can fly, but a cow cannot fly.

A fish can swim, but a cow can’t swim.

Could is the past tense of can. In this song, it is used to expresses ability in the past.

Write these examples on the board:

When I was a very small baby, I couldn’t talk.

I am an adult now, so I can talk.

When I was a child, I couldn’t drive a car.

I am adult now, so I can drive a car.

You can find information on can and could on pages 354 and 362 in Chapter 12 of Basic

English Grammar

31. Who is __ the baby for you?


Who is babysitting the baby for you?

Artinya: Siapa yang mengasuh bayinya untukmu?

PEMBAHASAN

Pada kata babysitting, kata dasarnya adalah babysit.

Babysit terjemahan Bahasa Indonesianya adalah mengasuh anak atau menjaga anak.

Mengapa digunakan kata '-ing' pada akhir kata?

Digunakan kata 'ing' pada akhir verb (kata kerja) karena terdapat kata 'is' di kalimat.

Present Continuous

Tense yang digunakan pada kalimat tersebut adalah present continuous.

Present continuous digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah kejadian sedang berlangsung sekarang.

Struktur Kalimat Present Continuous:

(+) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + verb 1 + '-ing'(-) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + not + verb 1 + '-ing'(?) To be (is/am/are) + subject + verb 1 + '-ing'

Contoh Kalimat Present Continuous:

(+) She is eating.(-) She is not eating.(?) Is she eating?

Penjelasan Contoh Kalimat di Atas:

"She is eating" menyatakan bahwa dia (perempuan) sedang makan."She is not eating" menyatakan bahwa dia (perempuan) sedang tidak makan."Is she eating?" menanyakan bahwa apakah dia (perempuan) sedang makan.

TAMBAHAN

Subyek dan To Be:

Subyek i menggunakan AM.

Subyek he, she, it menggunakan IS.

Subyek they, we, you menggunakan ARE.

Verb 1:

Verb dalam Bahasa Indonesia artinya kata kerja.

Verb 1 biasanya juga dibilang sebagai kata kerja dasar.

Contoh verb 1 adalah drink.

Jika 'drink' ditambah akhiran '-ing', maka akan berubah menjadi 'drinking'.

Notes for the Teacher

1. The song

Do a search on the Internet to find the song “I Believe I Can Fly” by R. Kelly. When you

search, be sure to include the title and the name of the artist.

2. Song background

R. Kelly is an R & B (rhythm and blues) singer and songwriter. He first sang in subway

stations in Chicago, Illinois, but now he is very famous. In fact, he was one of the most

successful R & B performers of the 1990s.

R. Kelly first sang this song, “I Believe I Can Fly,” in 1996. R. Kelly says that he wrote

the song for Michael Jordon, a very famous basketball player who jumps very high. The

song is inspirational -- it tries to help people succeed in life.

3. Grammar background

This song uses the modal verb can to expresses ability.

Write these examples on the board. Point out to students that can is followed by the

simple form of the verb.

A bird can fly, but a cow cannot fly.

A fish can swim, but a cow can’t swim.

Could is the past tense of can. In this song, it is used to expresses ability in the past.

Write these examples on the board:

When I was a very small baby, I couldn’t talk.

I am an adult now, so I can talk.

When I was a child, I couldn’t drive a car.

I am adult now, so I can drive a car.

You can find information on can and could on pages 354 and 362 in Chapter 12 of Basic

English Grammar
