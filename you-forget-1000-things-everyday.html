Pembahasan

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang menunjukan keterangan waktu di masa lalu. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V2

I watched TV last week

He met his wife 10 years ago

(-) Subject + did NOT + V1

She didn’t finish the exercise yesterday

I didn’t go to school last week

(?) Did + Subject + V1?

Did you see me yesterday?

Did Bella take her medicine?

Simple past tense of to be, to have, to do

I, He/She/It => was, had, did

You, We, They => were, had, did
