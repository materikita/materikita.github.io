Receptionist : Okay, could you please show me your ID card, Sir?

Mr. Dean : Here it is. Oh, is it possible for me to have a room with a shower, instead of a bath?

Receptionist : Sure. Any other, Sir?

Mr. Dean : I am going to pay the bill for my room now. So, can I have my bill?

Receptionist : One second, Sir. You will have your bill right away.

Dialogue 2:

Tour guide : Good afternoon, Sir! What can I help you?

Mr. Kim : Yes. I want to watch a movie in the theater in this town. I cannot find any. Can you show me, please?

Tour guide : Sure. There are two movie theaters in this town, Sir. One is in the Honeycomb Street. It is around 8 km from here. The other is in Papaya Street.

Mr. Kim : Papaya Street? I found that street when I was taking a walk to here.

Tour guide : Yes, Sir. It is only two km from here.They don't have the long queue just like the one in the Honeycomb Street.

Mr. Kim : I think I will go there, but I have a big family with me. I think I need to hire a car. Do you provide it?

Tour guide : Sure. What type of car do you need?

Mr. Kim : Anything that fits on my big family.

Tour guide : I'll show you the units that we have. This way, Sir.

Mr. Kim : Okay, right after you.

Dialogue 3:

Zea : Good Day. Is it possible for me to talk to the manager, please? I need to book some seats for a birthday party.

Manager : My ears are here for you, Miss.

Zea : Okay, I will throw a birthday party here. It will be for 25 guests. Can I have the private room, please?

Manager : Sure. Do you want the indoor or outdoor venue, Miss?

Zea : Outdoor will be great.

Manager : I need your help to fill in this form, please.

Zea : Sure. Here we go.

Manager : Okay, we will prepare everything that you ask for. Is there anything else, Miss Zea?

Zea : I think I will take my lunch in here now. Can I have a seat for 3, please?

Manager : Sure. Follow me.
