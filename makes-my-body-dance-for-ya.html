12. jadikan kalimat interogatif dari kata my father makes fried rice for breakfast​


Jawaban

(?) Does my father make fried rice for breakfast?

Penjelasan

Kalimat di atas menggunakan Present Simple Tense.

Present Simple Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan :

- Kejadian yang terjadi sekarang

- Kebenaran umum

- Aksi kebiasaan

Rumus Present Simple Tense (Kalimat Verbal)

1. Untuk Subject I, You, We, They

(+) Subject + Verb 1 + Object

(-) Subject + "don't" + Verb 1 + Object

(?) "Do" + Subject + Verb 1 + Object

2. Untuk Subject He, She, It

(+) Subject + Verb 1 + "s/es" + Object

(-) Subject + "doesn't" + Verb 1 + Object

(?) "Does" + Subject + Verb 1 + Object

32. She is my mother. She always .... delicious cakes for my family.A. Make B. Made C. MakesD. Making​


Jawaban:

C. Makes

Penjelasan:

Simple Present Tense:

Adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berfrekuensi (teratur) seperti sehari sekali, seminggu sekali, setiap hari, dsb.

Adverb of Frequency:

1. Always = Selalu

2. Constantly = Selalu

3. Usually, normally = biasanya

4. Frequently = sering

5. Regularly = secara teratur.

6. Often = sering

7. Sometimes = Kadang-kadang

8. Occasionally, infrequently, seldom = Sekali-sekali.

9. Rarely/hardly ever = Jarang.

10. Never = tidak pernah

Penggunaan simple present tense pada kalimat positif

Contoh :

I, you, they, we :

I always go to school at 7 o'clock

You often bring meal from your house.

They seldom greet each other.

We never get late for school.

He,she,it

He rarely use(s) his cap.

She frequently go(es) to the mall.

My cat always chase(s) rat every time it saw it.
