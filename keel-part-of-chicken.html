8. 5. Yaya bought 5 portion of chicken. The mass of each portion of chicken was 2 kg. Each kg of rice cost Rp 24.000,00. How much did Yaya pay fot the 5 portion of chicken? ​


Jawaban:

To calculate the total cost of the 5 portions of chicken, we need to find the cost per portion of chicken and then multiply it by the number of portions.

Given that each portion of chicken weighs 2 kg, we can find the total mass of chicken by multiplying the weight of each portion (2 kg) by the number of portions (5):

Total mass of chicken = 2 kg/portion * 5 portions = 10 kg

Now, we can find the cost of the 10 kg of chicken. However, the price of chicken is not provided, so we cannot calculate the cost accurately. If you provide the price per kg of chicken, I can help you calculate the cost of the 5 portions.

translate:

Untuk menghitung total biaya dari 5 porsi ayam, kita perlu mencari harga per porsi ayam tersebut dan kemudian mengalikannya dengan jumlah porsi.

Diketahui bahwa setiap porsi ayam memiliki berat 2 kg, kita dapat mencari total berat ayam dengan mengalikan berat setiap porsi (2 kg) dengan jumlah porsi (5):

Total berat ayam = 2 kg/porsi * 5 porsi = 10 kg

Sekarang, kita dapat mencari biaya untuk 10 kg ayam. Namun, harga ayam tidak diberikan, sehingga kita tidak dapat menghitung biaya dengan akurat. Jika Anda memberikan harga per kg ayam, saya dapat membantu Anda menghitung biaya dari 5 porsi tersebut.
