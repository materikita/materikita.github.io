8. you ...(not laugh) for my story and I .....(be)sad a.have not laughed,have beenb. has not laughed, has beenc. has laughed,has beend. have laughed,have been​


(a) have not laughed, have been

→ you have not laughed for my story and I have been sad.

[tex]\\[/tex]

PENDAHULUAN

Present perfect tense adalah kalimat untuk menyatakan suatu tindakan atau peristiwa yang bermula dari masa lalu namun masih kejadian/berhubungan hingga sekarang.

rumus:

(+) s + have/has + v³ + o/c(-) s + have/has + not + v³ + o/c(?) have/has + s + v³ + o/c + ?

tambahan:

have → untuk subjek I/you/we/theyhas → untuk subjek he/she/it

[tex]\\[/tex]

PEMBAHASAN

you ... (not laugh) for my story and I ..... (be) sad

you, I → haveverb 3 laugh → laughedverb 3 be → been

→ you have not laughed for my story and I have been sad.

[tex]\\[/tex]

KESIMPULAN

Jawabannya adalah opsi A (have not laughed, have been)

→ you have not laughed for my story and I have been sad.

15. Ubah kalimat dibawah menjadi kalimat langsung ke dalam bahasa inggris: 1. The fly then shouted angrily "oh bull, if you find that i am too heavy for you, let me know and i'll fly away 2. The bull laughed and said, " little fly , i don't care if you stay or leave. You are so tiny that your weight does not make any difference to me, so please be quite and leave me alone"


Subject : English
Category : Reported Speech

Yang perlu diingat saat membuat kalimat langsung menjadi kalimat tidak langsung :
1. Jika reporting verb dalam past tense, tense nya mundur. Present jadi Past, Past jadi Past Perfect.
2. I adalah yang bicara, YOU adalah yang diajak bicara.
3. Perubahan keterangan waktu.
4. Kalimat positif dimulai dengan (THAT). Kalimat tanya dimulai dengan IF/WHETHER (untuk yes-no question) dan KATA TANYA. Kalimat perintah dimulai dengan TO + infinitive.
5. This→that, these→those, here→there

ANSWERS :
1. The fly then shouted angrily to the bull that if the bull found that the fly was too heavy for him then the bull should let the fly know and he would fly away.

2. The bull laughed and said to the little fly that he didn't care if the fly stayed or left. He also said that the fly was so tiny that his weight did not make any difference to the bull, so the fly should just be quiet and leave the bull alone.
