Rumus:    

(+) To be / Modal / Auxiliary verb + Pronoun? (-)  To be / Modal / Auxiliary verb + Not + Pronoun?

Kalimat pada soal menggunakan pola kalimat simple past tense maka to be dan kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) yang digunakan juga harus dalam bentuk past.

Hal-hal yang harus diperhatikan dalam membuat kalimat question tags:

Statement positif diberi tag negatif. Jawaban positif yang diharapkan. Statement negatif diberikan tag positif. Jawaban negatif yang diharapkan.

7. Was she reading these lines?( turn into passive voice )a) these lines were being readb) she was being read these linesc) Were these lines being reading by her?d) Were these lines being read by her?Yok kakak" bantu akuu​


≡ Passive Voice ≡

(d) Were these lines being read by her?

Pembahasan

Passive voice merupakan bentuk kalimat yang subjeknya adalah penerima dari suatu aksi. Sementara itu, objeknya adalah pelaku dari aksi tersebut.

based on question

Past continuous tense

av → subject + was/were + verb-ing + O/C

pv → subject + was/were + being + verb 3 + by (agent)

–» active

Was she reading these lines?

subject = (was) sheverb = readingobject = these lines

–» passive

Were these lines being read by her?

subject = (were) these linesverb = (being) readobject = her

verb on question

Irregular verbs; kata kerja tidak beraturan.

verb 1 = readverb 2 = readverb 3 = readmeaning = “membaca”

answer:

➡ Were these lines being read by her? (d)

9. Explain:What's the difference: 1) reading the lines 2) reading between the lines 3) reading beyond the lines Give some example (Jelaskan dalam bahasa indonesia juga gak apa2)


The differences are:
1. This sentence states that you're reading the lines. This might/ could mean that you're reading a specific number of lines from a text, or you might be reading the whole text. Ex.: I'm reading the lines of the article.
2. This sentence states that you're reading or finding something, a diffrent or a more accurate or simplified meaning of a text, or both, but not what the text blatantly says or not as what the text literally says. Ex.: When you need to read a really long novel or a story in a limited amount of time, you need to read between the lines to get your job done quickly. This text functions almost the same as TL;DR or as they say: To Late; Don't Read.
3. This sentence means that you're reading what's not written in something. You're thinking outside the box. Ex.: To find the real meaning of a story, you need to read between and beyond the lines. Ex.2: Cleo needs to read beyond the lines to find another solution for his problem. Ex.3: Her mom reads of what's beyond the line and found her dirty secrets of all that she did by far.

19. apa yg dimaksud SDSL (symetrial digital suberiber lines) dan ADSL (asymetric digital subcriber lines)! apa perbedaan di antara keduanya!


=> ADSL atau Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line adalah salah satu bentuk dari teknologi DSL atau metode transmisi data digital berkecepatan tinggi melalui kabel tembaga. ADSL mampu mengirimkan data dengan kecepatan bit yang tinggi, berkisar antara 1.5 Mbps – 8 Mbps untuk arah downstream (sentral – pelanggan), dan antara 16 Kbps – 640 Kbps untuk arah upstream (pelanggan – sentral). Ciri khas ADSL adalah sifatnya yang asimetrik, yaitu bahwa data ditransferkan dalam kecepatan yang berbeda dari satu sisi ke sisi yang lain.
=> SDSL atau Symmetric Digital Subscriber Line adalah layanan akses Internet kecepatan tinggi dengan pencocokan upstream dan downstream kecepatan data. Artinya, data dapat dikirim ke Internet dari mesin klien atau diterima dari Internet dengan ketersediaan bandwidth yang sama di kedua arah.
