5. Bagaimana cara mengecek kapasitas pada flash drive?​


Jawaban:

Misalkan flash drive merk V-GEN, caranya:

- Langkah 1: tancapkan flash drive ke komputer/ laptop

- Langkah 2: pastikan flash drive terdeteksi komputer/ laptop

- Langkah 3: masuk ke dalam Windows Explorer

- Langkah 4: klik kanan flash drive (dg mouse) dan klik Properties

- Langkah 5: Amati jendela yg tampil (menunjukkan kapasitas flash drive)

Penjelasan tambahan:

Untuk lebih jelasnya, amati lampiran (misalkan sudah masuk langkah 3):

- Langkah 4: ditunjukkan tanda panah biru

- Langkah 5: jendela yg tampil ditunjukkan tanda panah hijau

Pada jendela yg tampil itu, terdapat keterangan-keterangan:

→ Used space (kapasitas yg terpakai): 7,46 GB

→ Free space (kapasitas yg kosong): 65,1 MB

→ Capacity (kapasitas keseluruhan): 7,53 GB

11. Cara membuat animasi di adobe flash​


Jawaban:

1.Buatlah dokumen baru dengan nama tertentu, lakukan import gambar ke dalam library.

2.Dalam cara pembuatannya, pastikan file telah tersimpan dalam library sehingga akan lebih siap untuk digunakan di stage.

3.Lebih detailnya, import to library yang bisa dilakukan dengan memilih menu file, kemudian import dan to library.

4.Jika proses telah berhasil, maka anda akan menjumpai adanya gambar pada panel library. Untuk membuatnya disarankan mengganti nama layer 1 menjadi istilah karakter.

5.Masukkan gambar yang sebelumnya telah diimport dalam sebuah stage dengan klik serta menggesernya. Aturlah posisi gambar dengan memanfaatkan garis bantu berbentuk rules.

6.Lakukan pengaturan gambar pad acara membuat animasi sesuai dengan kebutuhan melalui penarikan ke kiri maupun ke kanan.

7.Jika pembuatan guide telah rampung, selanjutnya menambahkan blank key frame yang bisa dilakukan dengan memilih tombol F7 pada keyboard.

8.Selanjutnya masukkan gambar yang kedua jika langkah sebelumnya telah berhasil dibuat, dan pastikan memberi jeda berupa satu frame diantara gambar agar terlihat lebih sempurna. Untuk melihat hasilnya, anda bisa menekan tombol CTRL+Enter secara bersamaan

24. What is the moral lesson that you get from the movie "the flash"​


Life is about progress. Those who accept their mistakes and move forwards are those who succeed.

» PEMBAHASAN

Barry Allen is a human, and he makes mistakes too. He might have unmatched abilities compared to the rest of humanity, but therein the problem lies - initially he thought that by being fast he would be able to solve all of his problems and succeed at life. The opposite occurs. He gradually begins to realize that this thought-process is mistaken and that each circumstance faced in life will present a new problem, one where he will not be able to always make the correct decisions. An example of this at the end of Season 2, when he decides to run back in time to the night of his mother’s murder to take preventative measures which he thinks will grant (See the joke there? As in Grant Gustin? Never mind) him inner peace, albeit this is later revealed to be a disastrous decision once he returns to Earth-1 to find out that he has brought some of Flashpoint’s metas with him, including The Rival and especially Dr Alchemy, who serves the self-proclaimed God of Speed, Savitar. You see here, Barry thought everything would be just the way it had been once he returned from Flashpoint. However, all actions have consequences. Now, Cisco’s brother Dante is dead, Iris doesn’t talk to Joe any longer and as mentioned above, a mysterious Dr Alchemy has entered the scene and is causing unparalleled levels of chaos by granting certain individuals inhuman abilities. Barry is now not only distanced from his friends but has just allowed entry of who is the possibly the most powerful villain yet - one who moves fast enough to be invisible to ordinary humans. Imagine what Barry feels. His world is crumbling around him yet thankfully, he allows his sense of moral and social responsibility to persuade him to accept his situation and act on it, even if everyone is not exactly the same as they were pre-Flashpoint. We, as the viewers, are essentially taught to take responsibility for our actions and accept the outcomes. That is what we are instructed on a personal level. The underlying message through all of the seasons though is that we all must work together in order to make a positive impact on the world, and that being static to the change around us will only ruin us.

39. bagaimana cara membuat flash card


1. Satu hari maksimal 15 kartu dan dibagi per 5 kartu dan tiga kategori (Mis. keluarga, furniture dan hewan).

2. Jarak tiap kategori minimal 15 menit.

3. Nge-flash kartunya tidak boleh lama-lama, per kartu hanya 1 detik sambil kita diktekan kata-katanya lewat contekan dibelakang kartu tersebut.

4. Nge-flash kartunya dimulai dari yang paling belakang ke arah depan, bukan sebaliknya.

5. Tiap kartu di flash maksimal 3 kali selama satu hari dan hanya dipakai selama 5 hari. Setelah itu kartu kadaluarsa.

6. Tiap kali mau nge-flash (5 kartu), kartu-kartu tersebut harus dikocok terlebih dahulu alias jangan selalu berurutan nge-flashnya.
