PembahasanYes / No Questions

Yes / no questions adalah jenis pertanyaan yang paling mudah. Mereka dapat dijawab dengan menuliskan yes / no.

1. Are you a student? Yes, I am

2. Is Danise coming today? Yes, she is

3. Does she know the story? No, she doesn't

4. Will he come? No, he will not

5. Did he cry last night ? No, he didn't

More examples :

Statement : Chandra is a teacher.

Question : Is Chandra a teacher?

Present tense :

Statement : She lives in Jakata.

Question : Does she live in Jakarta?

Past tense :

Statement : He played football yesterday.

Question : Did he play football yesterday?
