Ubahlah kalimat ini menjadi kalimat indirect speech!Rumus : HE ASKED ME ---> WHEN/WHAT --->S + V + COMPLEMENT(1.) the student said to the teacher : we haven' t done our assignment."(2) My uncle said to my sister" i cannot come on your birthday party next month."(3.) Patar asked lina:"Do you love me?(4.) My father asked me : " Have you prepared for you final exam?"(5.) Anita told her : " Don't tell me more about your boyfriend?"(6.) My friend asked her : " stay with me forever !"(7.) My headmaster asked me :"where do you life ?"​


REPORTED SPEECH

(1) The student said to the teacher : we haven' t done our assignment."

↪ The students said to the teacher (that) they hadn't done their assignment.

(2) My uncle said to my sister" i cannot come on your birthday party next month."

↪ My uncle said to my sister (that) he couldn't come on her birthday party the following month.

(3.) Patar asked lina:"Do you love me?

↪ Patar asked Lina whether she loved him.

(4.) My father asked me : " Have you prepared for you final exam?"

↪ My father asked me whether I had prepared for my final exam.

(5.) Anita told her : " Don't tell me more about your boyfriend?"

↪ Anita forbade her to tell her more about her boyfriend.

or

↪ Anita warned her not to tell her more about her boyfriend.

(6.) My friend asked her : " stay with me forever !"

↪ My friend begged her to stay with him/her forever.

(7.) My headmaster asked me :"where do you life ?"

↪ My headmaster asked me where I lived.

EXPLANATION

Reported speech/indirect speech adalah kalimat berita, ungkapan pelaporan ulang dari apa yang telah disampaikan oleh narasumber kepada orang lain tanpa mengubah informasi tersirat.

Hal² yang diperhatikan dalam merubah direct speech ke reported speech :

~ Cermati jenis kalimatnya, apakah termasuk afirmasi, tanya dan imperatif [saran, nasihat, larangan, perintah].

~ Perhatikan sudut pandang.

~ Perhatikan jenis reporting verb yg diminta; pakai bentuk PAST [told, asked, warned] atau PRESENT [tell, ask, warn].

Jika menggunakan "Present simple, Present perfect, Future", noun clause tdk mengalami perubahan tenses.

Sebaliknya, jika reporting verb berbentuk past {said, told, asked, warned}, noun clause verb mengalami backshift

baik dari keterangan waktu, petunjuk maupun tenses.

Simple present ➛Simple past

Simple past ➛Past perfect

Present perfect ➛Past perfect

Past continuous ➛Past perfect continuous

Now ➛at that time

Today ➛ that day

Yesterday ➛ the day before

Tomorrow ➛ the next day

Last week ➛ the previous week

Example :

D.S : Bandung Bondowso told his genies, "Don't do everything slowly."

R.S : Bandung Bondowoso forbade his genies not to do everything slowly.

~ Reporting verb yg digunakan adalah bentuk past "told" dan kalimatnya berupa kalimat larangan. Maka, gunakan "forbade" atau "warned" utk mendeklamasikan noun clause pada kalimat tak langsungnya.

~ Bentuk noun clause verb berupa to infinitive.

Greeting and Parting

Greeting atau dalam bahasa Indonesia disebut sapaan adalah ungkapan-ungkapan yang digunakan dalam menyapa saat bertemu dengan seseorang.

Ungkapan greeting antara lain:

Hello Hi Good morning How is going? How are you?

Parting adalah ungkapan yang digunakan saat kita akan berpisah dengan orang lain.

Ungkapan Parting dalam Bahasa Inggris adalah:

See you later See you tomorrow Good bye Bye Nice to see you

Berikut ini adalah perbandingan Formal dan Informal dalam greeting dan parting

Formal VS Informal

Hi = Informal

Good morning = Formal

How have you been = Informal

How are you = Formal

How are you doing = Formal

NB: Formal: Resmi/sopan, Informal: tidak resmi/ kurang sopan

Synonym dari beberapa ungkapan greeting dan parting adalah:

Hi = Hello

How is going? = How are you?

Fine, thanks = Very well, thank you

Where are you going to? = Where you off to?

Good luck = All the best

See you later = Good bye
