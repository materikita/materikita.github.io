7. Describe about your school


Descriptive TextAnswer

My name is Dew Barrymore. I’m thirteen years old. I study at SMPN I “Brainly Nuansa Indah.” My school is elite and very famous in my town. Many students come from different regions to study in this school.

(Identification)

My school is green and shady. There are variety of plants and flowers. The trees produce the fresh air all over the school building and it makes more comfortable to stay. My school has many rooms.  There are a spacious library, a large parking shade, a headmaster’s office, school canteens, language laboratories, science laboratories, multimedia rooms, toilets, a hall and twenty rooms for seventh grade class until ninth grade class. The school yard is very large. It is in front of the ninth grade classes. We can play kinds of sport games in the school yard. My school has complete sport equipment  because my school provides the sport clubs to train for those who are talented in the sport game.  ( Description )

When we enter the school,   we will see the greeting billboard which is written “Welcome to SMPN I “Brainly Nuansa Indah”  in front of the school building gate. There is a security post beside the gate. There are two securities  who guard the post. They have responsibility to keep our school safe. That is a story about my school. I love my school very much because of its conducive environment. ( Description )

Explanation

Descriptive teks merupakan teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik.

Social Function/Communicative Purpose

to describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

Generic Structure

1.  Identification  

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di awal sebagai perujukkan, pengenalan, penjelas informasi dari siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks

2. Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya yang menjadi identik dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

Language Features

1. Penggunaan kata benda/hal yang spesifik

Example : my dream, my house, mount merapi, Taj Mahal , Borobudur

2. Penggunaan  kata sifat ( adjective )

Kata sifat  digunakan dalam penjabaran karakter khusus dari hal yang dibicarakan.

3. Penggunaan pola simple present tense

Secara garis besar, struktur kalimat dalam teksnya menggunakan simple present tense karena sifat informasinya fakta/nyata/apa adanya.

5. Penggunaan kata kerja aksi

Kata kerja aksi memuat informasi tindakan.
