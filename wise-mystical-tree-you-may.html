2. what is photosynthesis?@Mystical.....I'm Purnima ​


Photosynthesis is the process of making food in plants. Photosynthesis takes place in the leaves and plant bodies that contain chlorophyll. Photosynthesis requires sunlight, chlorophyll, water and carbon dioxide.

water is absorbed by the roots from the soil. Water from the roots is lifted by the wooden vessels to the leaves.

Carbon dioxide is absorbed from the air by the leaves through the leaf mouths (stomata).

Through photosynthesis, water and carbon dioxide are then converted into carbohydrates and oxygen with the help of sunlight energy.

If sunlight energy is not available, other light energy can replace it. Therefore, photosynthesis can occur during the day or night as long as there is light.

May I go to the toilet, miss?

ANSWER

Amongst the given choices, the correct answer is letter D. No. not now.
