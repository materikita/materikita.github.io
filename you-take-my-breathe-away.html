17. Ubah kalimat aktif menjadi pasif my brother will call you right away


Jawaban:

You will be called by my brother right away

Penjelasan:

PASSIVE VOICE FOR SIMPLE FUTURE

Formula :

---> Subject + will be + V3 + by + Objective Pronouns

Example(s) :

1. Jen will write the report

The report will be written by Jen

2. My mom will sit on this chair

This chair will be sat by my mom
