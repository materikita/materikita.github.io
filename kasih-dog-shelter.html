1. Caleb is a volunteer at the animal shelter. The animal shelter has only cats and dogs. 3/10 of the animals are dog. There are 8 fewer dogs than cats. How many animals are there in the animal shelter. ​


the cat ratio = 10 - 3 = 7

The animals in the shelter

[tex] \frac{(cat + dog)ratio}{(cat - dog)ratio} \times (cat - dog) \: \\ \\ \frac{(7 + 3)}{(7 - 3)} \times 8 \: animals \\ \\ \frac{10}{4} \times 8 \: animals \\ \\ 20 \: animals[/tex]

29. There are some dogs live in the shelter. Due to illness, 2 dogs passed on. There are 9 children adopted each y dog. 1/5 of the dogs left in the shelter. How many are the remaining dogs? (Write down in algebraic expression)


Total of dogs = passed away dogs + adopted dogs + left dogs

Assume: total of dogs = X
X = 2 + 9 + (1/5) X
X - (1/5) X = 11
(4/5) X = 11
X = 55/4 = 13,75

13,75 dogs or we can assume 14 dogs
