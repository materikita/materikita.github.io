6. 40 POINT JAWAB CEPET SEBELUM JAM 12 DIKUMPUL JANGAN NGASAL UBAH KE NEGATIVE DAN INTROGATIVE -When i cried, they CALMED me -down. when I WAS afraid of -something, they COMFORTED me. -my dad often CARRIED me around in -the morning sun. when i GOT older, -they SPOOFED me. i PLAYED with -my brother. I WAS naughty, but he -never GOT angry with me. he WAS -very patient with me


Jawaban:

1. Negative: When I cried, they didn't calm me down

Interrogative: Did they calm you down when you were crying?

2. Negative: When I was afraid of something, they didn't comfort me

Interrogative: Did they comfort you when you were afraid of something?

3. Negative: My dad didn't often carry me around in the morning sun

Interrogative: Did your dad often carry you around in the morning sun?

4. Negative: When I got older, they didn't spoof me

Interrogative: Did they spoof you when you were getting older?

5. Negative: I didn't play with my brother

Interrogative: Did you play with your brother?

6. Negative: I was naughty, but he got angry with me

Interrogative: Was he got angry when you was naughty?

7. Negative: He wasn't very patient with me

Interrogative: Was he very patient with you?

Tujuan dari Recount Text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau untuk menghibur pembaca sehingga tidak terdapat konflik.

Orientation : berisi tentang pengenalan mengenai latar belakang cerita, kapan , dimana dan orang-orang yang trerlibat di dalam cerita  Event :merupakan rangkaian kejadian di dalam cerita yang di ceritakan secara kronologis  Reorientation : kesimpulan yang merupakan komentar penulis mengenai kejadian.  

Language features:  

Menggunakan noun dan pronoun, contoh: I, he, we, they  Menggunakan action verbs , contoh: went, played, spent  Menggunakan time conjunction, contoh: and, but, after, dan finally  Menggunakan adverbs dan adverbs of phrases, contoh: in my house, two days ago, slowly  Menggunakan adjective, contoh: brave, strong, smart  Menggunakan past tense
