6. During a swimming carnival, Gerald swam a distance of 40 m at a speed

of 1 m/s. Then he swam for another 40 seconds at a speed of 1.5 m/s

He swam a total distance of 100 m.

a Find the total time taken.

Total Time: Time 1 + Time 2

= Time 1: 40 m x 1 m/s

= 40 sec

= Time 2: 40 sec

Total time taken: 40 + 40 = 80 seconds

b What was Gerald's average speed for the whole distance?​

Average Speed: Total Distance ÷ Total Time taken

= 100 m ÷ 80

= 1.25 m/sec

6. farhan cycles the first part of 150km journey at an average speed of 35 km/h and walks the remaining distance at an average speed of 5 km/h. if he takes 4,5 hours for his entire journey, find the distance that he cycles


Time 1 (First part)
= 150km : 35km/h
= 4 2/7 h

Time 2 (Remaining distance)
= 4,5 - 4 2/7
= 4 1/2 - 4 2/7
= 4 7/14 - 4 4/14
= 3/14 h

Distance 1 = 150km

Distance 2
= Speed x Time
= 5 km/h x 3/14 h
= 1.07 km

Total Distance
= 150 + 1.07
= 151.07 km
