22. Change the sentences into imperative. Do like the example given You want you friend not to disturb you. Answer : don’t disturb You want your friend to be on time. Answer : be on time ! 1. You want your friend to do home work on time 2. You want your friend not to cheat you 3. You want your friend to tidy his bed 4. You want your friend to brush his teeth 5. You want your friend to respect teachers 6. You want your friend not to be naughty 7. You want your friend not to be late 8. You want your friend not to wash hand as many as possible 9. You want your friend to be careful 10. You want your friend not to be afraid


IMPERATIVE SENTENCE

1. You want your friend to do home work on time

➡ Do your homework on time!

2. You want your friend not to cheat you

➡ Don't cheat me!

3. You want your friend to tidy his bed

➡ Make up your bed!

or

➡ Tidy up your bed!

4. You want your friend to brush his teeth

➡ Brush your teeth!

5. You want your friend to respect teachers

➡ Respect your teachers!

6. You want your friend not to be naughty

➡ Don't be naughty!

7. You want your friend not to be late

➡ Don't be late!

8. You want your friend not to wash hand as many as possible

➡ Don't wash your hand as many as possible!

9. You want your friend to be careful

➡ Be careful!

10. You want your friend not to be afraid

➡ Don't be afraid!

EXPLANATION

Imperative sentence adalah kalimat/ungkapan yg digunakan utk menyuruh, memerintah, melarang, meminta seseorang utk melakukan sesuatu.

Ciri-ciri kalimat imperatif:

~ Tidak diawali dengan subyek kalimat tetapi kata kerja dasar/Verb ¹.

~ Menggunakan tanda exclamation (!) utk mempertegas intonasinya.

Jenis kalimat imperatif :

[1] Kalimat perintah [Command] adalah kalimat yg berisi perintah dan larangan.

Exp.

~ Be careful! (Hati-hati)

~ Tidy up your bed (Rapikan kasurmu)

~ Respect your teacher! (Hormati gurumu)

~ Be quite please! (Tlong diam donk)

~ Don't be naughty! (Jangan nakal)

[2] Kalimat permintaan [Request] adalah kalimat yg berisi permohonan utk melakukan sesuatu scr halus.

Exp.

~ Could you crack these eggs ? (Bisakah kmu memecahkan telur² ini)

~ Would you mind sending this letter to Mr. John? (Maukah kamu mengantarkan surat ini ke Pak John)
